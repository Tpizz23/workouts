Legs + Shoulders + Core

Saturday · 2026-06-06 · Full-Gym

45–60 min workoutBulk focusProgressive overload

Warm-Up — 5 min

  • Bodyweight squats and hip openers — 2 min
  • Glute bridges — 2 × 12
  • Shoulder circles plus light lateral raises — 60 sec
  • Brace practice: dead bug or plank breathing — 60 sec

Workout

Legs

Hack Squat Machine

Squat on the hack machine for a guided, quad-focused pattern.

3 sets8–12 repsGuided quad-heavy squat

Romanian Deadlift

Hinge with a barbell or dumbbells to stretch hamstrings and load glutes.

3 sets10–12 repsHamstring and glute hinge

Walking DB Lunge

Step forward through lunges with dumbbells and controlled knee tracking.

3 sets10–12/leg repsDynamic single-leg work

Seated Calf Raise

Raise heels from a seated machine to target the soleus calf muscle.

4 sets12–15 repsBent-knee calf emphasis

Shoulders

Barbell or DB Overhead Press

Press a barbell or dumbbells overhead from shoulder height with a braced torso.

4 sets8–10 repsOverhead strength anchor

Machine Lateral Raise

Use the lateral raise machine to isolate side delts with stable form.

3 sets12–15 repsStable side-delt pump

Face Pull to External Rotation

Pull the rope to your face and rotate hands back for rear delts and cuff.

3 sets12–15 repsRear delt and cuff health

Core

Pallof Press (cable)

Press a cable straight out and resist rotation through your trunk.

3 sets12/side repsAnti-rotation stability

Ab Wheel Rollout

Roll the wheel forward while keeping ribs down, then pull back with abs.

3 sets8–12 repsAnti-extension strengthBodyweight

Cool-Down / Stretch — 2 min

  • Couch or quad stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Hamstring fold — 45 sec/side
  • Cross-body shoulder stretch and slow breathing — 60 sec
