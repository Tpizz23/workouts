Back + Biceps

Wednesday, 2026-07-08 · starts 10:30 AM

Full-Gym45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbRest: 60–90s isolation, 2–3m compounds

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Hip hinges, cat-cow, and thoracic rotations for 60 seconds
  • Two easy row or pulldown ramp sets before working weight
  • Light curls plus scapular retractions to warm elbows and upper back

Workout

Back

Pull-Up or Weighted Pull-Up

Pull from a dead hang until your chin clears the bar, adding weight if bodyweight is easy.

4 sets6–10 repsBodyweight

Note: Full dead hang to chin over bar

Meadows Row

Row a landmine bar one arm at a time from a staggered stance for a strong lat angle.

3 sets10/side reps

Note: Landmine one-arm row for lat/upper-back angle

Straight-Arm Pulldown

Keep arms mostly straight and sweep the cable bar down using your lats.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Lat isolation without biceps takeover

Face Pull (cable, rope)

Pull the rope toward your face with elbows high to train rear delts and upper back.

3 sets15 reps

Note: External rotation, rear delts

Biceps

Incline DB Curl

Curl dumbbells from an incline bench to stretch the long head of the biceps.

3 sets12 reps

Note: Long head stretch on incline bench

Cable Hammer Curl (rope)

Curl a rope attachment with neutral grip for constant brachialis tension.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Constant-tension neutral-grip curl

EZ-Bar Reverse Curl

Curl an EZ-bar palms-down to hit forearms and brachialis.

3 sets12 reps

Note: Forearm and brachialis emphasis

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Child’s pose with side reach, 30 seconds per side
  • Cross-body rear delt stretch, 30 seconds per side
  • Gentle biceps wall stretch, 20 seconds per side

Progressive Overload Log

When all sets hit the top of the rep range with clean form, increase the load by 5 lb next time.
