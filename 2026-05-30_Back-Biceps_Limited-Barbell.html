Back + Biceps

Saturday · 2026-05-30 · Limited-Barbell

45–60 min workoutBulk focusProgressive overload

Warm-Up — 5 min

  • Cat-cow plus thoracic rotations — 60 sec
  • Hip-hinge drill — 2 × 10
  • Prone W squeezes — 2 × 10
  • Two lighter ramp-up row/pull sets before working weight

Workout

Back

Chest-Supported DB Row on Couch

Lie chest-down on an incline couch or bench substitute and row the dumbbells without swinging.

4 sets8–12 repsStrict row anchor

DB Bent-Over Row

Hinge at the hips and row both dumbbells toward your ribs with a strong back squeeze.

4 sets8–10 repsHeavy hinge row

DB Pullover Lat Focus

Lower one dumbbell behind your head and pull it back using your lats with soft elbows.

3 sets10–12 repsLat isolation

Prone Y-T-W Raise

Lie face-down and raise your arms into Y, T, and W shapes to light up rear delts and traps.

3 sets8 each shape repsUpper-back controlBodyweight

Biceps

DB Zottman Curl

Curl with palms up, rotate at the top, and lower palms-down to train biceps and forearms.

3 sets10–12 repsCurl up supinated, lower palms-down

Cross-Body Hammer Curl

Curl each dumbbell diagonally toward the opposite shoulder with a neutral grip.

3 sets10–12/side repsBrachialis emphasis

DB Drag Curl

Drag the dumbbells up your torso as your elbows move behind you for a strict bicep squeeze.

3 sets10–12 repsElbows travel back

Cool-Down / Stretch — 2 min

  • Child’s pose with lat reach — 45 sec/side
  • Cross-body rear delt stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Forearm/bicep wall stretch — 30 sec/side

Record working weight or reps. If all sets hit the top of the range cleanly, add 5 lb next time.
