💪 Workout

Legs

DB Goblet Squat
Hold one dumbbell at chest height and squat deep with a tall torso.
4 sets10–12 repsWeight (lb)
DB Romanian Deadlift
Hinge back with soft knees to stretch hamstrings and load the posterior chain.
3 sets10–12 repsWeight (lb)
DB Walking Lunge
Step forward under control and alternate legs for dynamic single-leg volume.
3 sets12/leg repsWeight (lb)
DB Sumo Squat
Use a wide stance and sit between the hips to hit glutes and inner thighs.
3 sets12 repsWeight (lb)
Jump Squat (bodyweight)
Explode up from a squat and land softly to finish legs with power work.
3 sets10 repsBodyweight

Shoulders

DB Push Press
Use a small knee dip to drive heavier dumbbells overhead with control.
3 sets8–10 repsWeight (lb)
DB Lateral Raise 1.5 Reps
Raise, lower halfway, raise again, then lower fully for extended side-delt tension.
3 sets10 repsWeight (lb)
DB Shrug
Pull shoulders straight up and pause at the top to train upper traps.
3 sets12–15 repsWeight (lb)

Core

Hollow Body Hold
Hold a rounded, braced position with low back pressed into the floor.
3 sets30 sec repsBodyweight
Plank Shoulder Tap
Tap shoulders from a plank while resisting hip rotation.
3 sets20 taps repsBodyweight
