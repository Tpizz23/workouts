Back + Biceps

Wednesday, 2026-07-08 · starts 10:30 AM

Limited-Barbell45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbRest: 60–90s isolation, 2–3m compounds

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Hip hinges, cat-cow, and thoracic rotations for 60 seconds
  • Two easy row or pulldown ramp sets before working weight
  • Light curls plus scapular retractions to warm elbows and upper back

Workout

Back

Chest-Supported DB Row on Couch

Lie chest-down on a couch or incline surface and row dumbbells without swinging.

4 sets8–12 reps

Note: Lie chest-down on an incline couch/bench substitute

DB Pullover Lat Focus

Use soft elbows and pull from the lats as the dumbbell travels from behind your head.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Keep elbows soft and pull from lats

Single-Arm DB Rear Delt Row

Row a dumbbell wide toward your shoulder to hit rear delts and upper back.

3 sets12/side reps

Note: Row wide toward shoulder to hit rear delts

Prone Y-T-W Raise

Lie prone and raise your arms through Y, T, and W shapes for upper-back control.

3 sets8 each shape repsBodyweight

Note: Bodyweight upper-back and shoulder control

Biceps

DB Zottman Curl

Curl palms-up, rotate at the top, and lower palms-down to train biceps and forearms.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Curl up supinated, lower palms-down

DB Drag Curl

Pull elbows back as the dumbbells slide up your torso for a strict biceps squeeze.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Pull elbows back as the dumbbells travel up torso

Cross-Body Hammer Curl

Curl each dumbbell toward the opposite shoulder with a neutral grip.

3 sets10–12/side reps

Note: Curl toward opposite shoulder for brachialis emphasis

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Child’s pose with side reach, 30 seconds per side
  • Cross-body rear delt stretch, 30 seconds per side
  • Gentle biceps wall stretch, 20 seconds per side

Progressive Overload Log

When all sets hit the top of the rep range with clean form, increase the load by 5 lb next time.
