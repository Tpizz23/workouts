🦵 Legs + Shoulders + Core

Saturday, April 11, 2026  ·  Limited-Barbell (Home)
🏠 Limited-Barbell ⏱ 45–60 min 🎯 Bulk: 165 → 180 lb
🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)
  • Bodyweight squat × 10 — air squat to activate quads and glutes
  • Walking lunges × 8/leg — dynamic hip mobility and leg activation
  • Standing calf raise × 15 — warm up the calves before loading
  • Arm circles forward and backward × 10 each — shoulder mobility
  • DB goblet squat hold × 30 sec — leg and core activation with the load
🏋️ Workout
LEGS (4 exercises)
DB Goblet Squat
Hold a dumbbell vertically at your chest like a goblet, then squat down between your legs keeping your elbows inside your knees, then stand back up.
4 sets 10–12 reps
Primary compound for legs — go heavy and aim for full depth (hip crease below knee). Keep your chest up and elbows tracking between your knees.
Weight (lb):
DB Bulgarian Split Squat
Stand with the instep of one foot on a chair or bench behind you, lower your back knee toward the floor in a split-squat position, then drive through your front heel to return.
3 sets 8–10 reps/leg
Front foot far enough forward that your knee stays over your toes. Rear foot elevated on the bench for balance. Squeeze your glute at the top.
Weight (lb):
DB Romanian Deadlift
Stand holding dumbbells in front of your thighs, push your hips back while keeping your legs nearly straight, lower the weights along your shins, then drive your hips forward to return.
3 sets 10–12 reps
Hip hinge — not a squat. Keeps legs mostly straight to isolate the hamstrings and glutes. Feel the stretch in your hamstrings at the bottom.
Weight (lb):
DB Single-Leg Calf Raise
Stand on one leg on the edge of a step with your heel hanging off, then lower your heel below the step and rise up on your toes as high as possible.
3 sets 15–20 reps/leg
Full range of motion — drop the heel well below the step for a deep stretch at the bottom. Hold a dumbbell in the opposite hand for added resistance.

SHOULDERS (3 exercises)
DB Overhead Press
Stand or sit with dumbbells at shoulder height, then press them straight overhead until your arms are fully extended, then lower back to the start position.
4 sets 8–10 reps
Primary compound for shoulders. Keep your core braced and avoid excessive back arch. Drive through your whole hand, not just your fingers.
Weight (lb):
DB Lateral Raise
Stand with dumbbells at your sides and raise your arms out to the sides in a wide arc until they reach shoulder height, elbows slightly bent.
3 sets 12–15 reps
Slight forward lean and pinky finger slightly higher than your thumb at the top. No swinging — control the weight on the way down.
Weight (lb):
DB Rear Delt Fly
Bend over at the hips with a slight bend in your elbows, then raise the dumbbells out to the sides in an arc, squeezing your shoulder blades together at the top.
3 sets 12–15 reps
Targets the rear deltoids and upper back. Keep your chest up and eyes down to maintain a flat back. Squeeze the shoulder blades together at the top.
Weight (lb):
CORE (2 exercises)
DB Russian Twist
Sit with your knees bent and feet off the floor, holding a dumbbell at your chest, then rotate your torso side to side, touching the weight to the floor beside each hip.
3 sets 20 reps (10/side)
Feet stay elevated throughout. Keep your core braced and rotate from yourobliques, not your arms. Slow and controlled — no momentum.
Weight (lb):
Plank
Hold a push-up position with your forearms flat on the floor, body in a perfectly straight line from head to heels, bracing your core hard throughout.
3 sets 45–60 sec Bodyweight
Do not let your hips sag or pike. Brace as if someone were about to punch you in the stomach. Breathe normally — don't hold your breath.
❄️ Cool-Down / Stretch (2 min)
  • Quad stretch — standing, pull your foot to your glute, 30 sec/side
  • Seated hamstring stretch — sit with one leg extended, reach for your toes, 30 sec/side
  • Calf stretch against the wall — 30 sec/side with a slight knee bend for the soleus
  • Cat-cow — 5 slow reps, exhale fully on the round back
  • Deep breathing — 5 slow, full belly breaths to close the session
