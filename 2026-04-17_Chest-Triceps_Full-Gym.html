Chest + Triceps
Build size with purpose today — strong compounds first, then clean hypertrophy work to finish the target muscles.
Equipment: Full-GymDuration: 45–60 minGoal: Bulk · Chest priorityStart: 3:30 PM
Warm-Up
  • 5 minutes of incline walk or easy rower, then band pull-aparts and arm circles for 2 rounds.
  • 2 progressive warm-up sets on bench press before the first heavy set.
  • 10 slow push-ups and 10 cable pressdowns to prime chest and elbows.
Workout

Chest

Barbell Bench Press

Press the bar from chest to lockout with your upper back tight and feet driving into the floor.

4 sets6–8 reps

Primary compound — heavy, progressive

Incline DB Press

Press dumbbells on a low incline to emphasize the upper chest through a long range.

3 sets8–10 reps

Upper chest, 30–45° incline

Cable Fly (low to high)

Bring the handles upward and inward in a hugging motion to fully shorten the chest.

3 sets12–15 reps

Full stretch and contraction

Pec Deck Machine

Sweep the pads together with soft elbows and squeeze the pecs hard at the midpoint.

3 sets12–15 reps

Squeeze hard at peak

Weighted Dips (chest lean)

Lean slightly forward and press out of the bottom to train the lower chest heavily.

3 sets8–12 reps

Lean forward to hit chest

Triceps

Cable Tricep Pushdown (rope)

Press the rope to full extension and split the ends apart at the bottom.

3 sets12–15 reps

Flare elbows out at bottom

EZ-Bar Skull Crusher

Lower the bar toward the forehead with control, then extend without shoulder swing.

3 sets10–12 reps

Controlled descent

Overhead Tricep Extension (cable)

Stretch the rope overhead and straighten the elbows to load the long head.

3 sets12–15 reps

Full stretch overhead

Cool-Down / Stretch
  • Doorway pec stretch and triceps overhead stretch for 30 seconds per side.
  • Foam-roll upper back for 60 seconds if available.
  • Walk slowly for 2 minutes and breathe through the nose before leaving.
