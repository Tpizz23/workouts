Back + Biceps

Full-Gym45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbProgressive overload focus

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Hip hinges with reach — 2 × 10
  • Scapular retractions — 2 × 12
  • Light rows/pulldowns — 2 ramp sets
  • Easy curls — 1 × 15 to warm elbows

Workout

Back

Pull-Up or Weighted Pull-Up

Pull from a dead hang until your chin clears the bar.

4 sets6–10

Vertical-pull strength anchor.

Barbell Bent-Over Row

Hinge and row a barbell to your torso with a strong back squeeze.

4 sets8–10

Heavy horizontal pull anchor.

Seated Cable Row (V-bar)

Pull a V-bar cable handle to your torso while driving elbows back.

3 sets10–12

Drive elbows back and squeeze.

Face Pull (cable, rope)

Pull rope handles toward your face with elbows high for rear delts and rotator cuff.

3 sets15

Rear-delt and shoulder-health finisher.

Biceps

Incline DB Curl

Curl dumbbells from a stretched position on an incline bench.

3 sets12

Long-head stretch curl.

Cable Curl (straight bar)

Curl a cable bar while keeping tension on the biceps the whole rep.

3 sets12–15

Constant-tension biceps work.

Hammer Curl

Curl dumbbells with a neutral grip to hit brachialis and forearms.

3 sets12–15

Neutral-grip arm finisher.

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Child’s pose with side reach — 45 sec/side
  • Cross-body rear delt stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Forearm/biceps wall stretch — 30 sec/side
