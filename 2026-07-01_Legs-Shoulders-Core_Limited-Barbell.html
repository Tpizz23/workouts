Legs + Shoulders + Core

Wednesday, 2026-07-01 · starts 12:00 PM

Limited-Barbell45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbRest: 60–90s isolation, 2–3m compounds

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Bodyweight squats, lunges, and hip circles for 90 seconds
  • Shoulder CARs and light lateral raises before pressing
  • Two ramp-up sets on the first leg movement

Workout

Legs

DB Front Squat

Hold dumbbells at shoulder height and squat with an upright torso.

4 sets8–12 reps

Note: Hold two dumbbells at shoulders for quad emphasis

DB Walking Lunge

Step forward into alternating lunges while holding dumbbells at your sides.

3 sets12/leg reps

Note: Dynamic lunge pattern for balance and volume

DB Single-Leg RDL

Hinge on one leg while lowering dumbbells and keeping hips square.

3 sets8–10/leg reps

Note: Balance-focused hamstring and glute movement

Shoulders

Seated DB Shoulder Press

Sit tall and press dumbbells overhead without using leg momentum.

4 sets8–10 reps

Note: Seated version to reduce leg drive

Lean-Away DB Lateral Raise

Lean from a support and raise one dumbbell to keep tension on the side delt.

3 sets10–12/side reps

Note: Hold support and lean away for longer side-delt tension

Prone Rear Delt Y-Raise

Lie face-down and lift light dumbbells in a Y to target rear delts and upper back.

3 sets12 reps

Note: Lie face-down and raise arms in a Y pattern

Core

DB Suitcase Carry/March

Hold one heavy dumbbell at your side and march while resisting leaning.

3 sets30 sec/side reps

Note: Hold one heavy DB and march or walk slowly

Bear Crawl Hold

Hold a tabletop position with knees hovering just off the floor while bracing hard.

3 sets30 sec repsBodyweight

Note: Knees hovering, brace and breathe

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Standing quad and hamstring stretches — 30 seconds/side
  • Figure-four glute stretch — 30 seconds/side
  • Doorway shoulder stretch and slow breathing

Progressive Overload Log

If you hit the top of the rep range with clean form for all sets, increase weight by 5 lb next time.
