Legs + Shoulders + Core

Limited-Barbell45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbProgressive overload focus

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Bodyweight squats — 2 × 12
  • Walking lunges — 1 × 10/side
  • Shoulder circles + light raises — 1 × 15
  • Dead bug breathing — 1 × 8/side

Workout

Legs

DB Front Squat

Hold two dumbbells at the shoulders and squat with a quad-focused upright torso.

4 sets8–12

Quad-focused squat anchor.

DB Single-Leg RDL

Balance on one leg and hinge with a dumbbell to train hamstrings and glutes.

3 sets8–10/leg

Balance-focused hinge.

DB Walking Lunge

Walk through alternating lunges while holding dumbbells for dynamic leg volume.

3 sets12/leg

Dynamic unilateral volume.

Wall Sit with DB

Hold a squat against the wall with a dumbbell for a quad-burning isometric.

3 sets30–45 sec

Quad burn finisher.

Shoulders

Seated DB Shoulder Press

Press dumbbells overhead while seated to reduce leg drive and isolate the shoulders.

4 sets8–10

Controlled press anchor.

DB Lateral Raise 1.5 Reps

Raise fully, lower halfway, raise again, then lower to extend side-delt time under tension.

3 sets10

Extra side-delt time under tension.

Prone Rear Delt Y-Raise

Lie face-down and raise arms in a Y to target rear delts and lower traps.

3 sets12

Rear-delt and lower-trap control.

Core

DB Suitcase Carry/March

Hold one dumbbell by your side and march or walk while resisting leaning.

3 sets30 sec/side

Anti-lean core strength.

Bear Crawl Hold

Bodyweight

Hover knees just off the floor while bracing and breathing.

3 sets30 sec

Brace hard with knees hovering.

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Couch hip-flexor stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Hamstring stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Cross-body shoulder stretch — 30 sec/side
  • Slow breathing in a dead bug position — 1 min
