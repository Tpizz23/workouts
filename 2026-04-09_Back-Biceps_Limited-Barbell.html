🔙 Back + Biceps

Thursday, April 9, 2026  ·  Limited-Barbell (Home)
🏠 Limited-Barbell ⏱ 45–60 min 🎯 Bulk: 165 → 180 lb
🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)
  • Cat-cow stretches — 30 sec, mobilizing the entire spine
  • Band pull-aparts or reverse snow angels × 10 — rear delt activation
  • DB swing or ghost deadlift × 10 — hip hinge pattern warm-up
  • Single-arm DB row hold × 5/side — scapula retraction activation
  • Bodyweight superman hold × 8 — lower back activation
🏋️ Workout
BACK (5 exercises)
DB Bent-Over Row
Hinge forward at the hips with a flat back, then pull the dumbbell toward your hip by driving your elbow back and squeezing your shoulder blade.
4 sets 8–10 reps
Primary compound — hinge at the hips, chest up, and pull with your elbow driving back. Alternate arms or do both together.
Weight (lb):
Single-Arm DB Row
Brace one hand and knee on a chair or couch, then pull the dumbbell straight up toward your hip, keeping your torso stable and parallel to the floor.
3 sets 10–12 reps
Full range of motion — let the shoulder blade protract at the bottom and retract at the top. Squeeze hard at the top.
Weight (lb):
DB Renegade Row
In a plank position with one dumbbell on the floor, row the dumbbell to your hip while maintaining a rigid, straight body line.
3 sets 8–10 reps
Core and back simultaneously — do not let your hips twist or sag. Brace hard through your entire midline.
Weight (lb):
DB Romanian Deadlift
Stand holding dumbbells in front of your thighs, push your hips back while keeping your legs nearly straight, lower the weights along your shins, then drive your hips forward to return.
3 sets 10–12 reps
Hip hinge pattern — not a squat. Keep your lower back neutral and feel the stretch in your hamstrings at the bottom.
Weight (lb):
Reverse Snow Angel (prone)
Lie face-down on the floor with arms extended overhead, squeeze your shoulder blades together and sweep your arms out to the sides and back down in a snow angel arc.
3 sets 12 reps Bodyweight
Rear delts and rhomboids — keep arms slightly bent and squeeze the shoulder blades together hard at the top.
BICEPS (4 exercises)
DB Curl
Stand with dumbbells at your sides and curl them up toward your shoulders by contracting your biceps, then lower back down with control.
3 sets 10–12 reps
Supinate (rotate wrists outward) at the top for a full contraction. No swinging — squeeze at the top for 1 second.
Weight (lb):
DB Hammer Curl
Curl the dumbbells up with a neutral grip (palms facing each other) keeping your elbows stationary at your sides.
3 sets 12–15 reps
Hits the brachialis and brachioradialis — these are the muscles that give your arm thickness below the bicep peak.
Weight (lb):
DB Concentration Curl
Sit with your elbow braced on the inside of your thigh and curl the dumbbell up to your shoulder, focusing on full contraction without body momentum.
3 sets 12 reps
Strict isolation — no body movement allowed. Full stretch at the bottom and a hard squeeze at the top of each rep.
Weight (lb):
DB Incline Curl
Lie back on a bench set to a low incline angle and let your arms hang straight down, then curl the dumbbells up to shoulder height.
2 sets 12 reps
The incline stretch hits the long head of the bicep — the part that gives the bicep its peak. No cheating on the eccentric.

❄️ Cool-Down / Stretch (2 min)
  • Doorframe lat stretch — arms extended in a doorframe, lean through for 30 sec
  • Child's pose — hold 30 sec, arms stretched forward, breathe into your back
  • Standing bicep stretch — pull one arm across chest with the other hand, 30 sec/side
  • Cat-cow cool-down — 5 slow reps to release spinal tension
  • 5 deep breaths — focus on expanding your upper back on the inhale
