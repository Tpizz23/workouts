💪 Chest + Triceps

Tuesday, April 7, 2026  ·  Limited-Barbell (Home)
🏠 Limited-Barbell ⏱ 45–60 min 🎯 Bulk: 165 → 180 lb ⚖️ Priority: Chest
🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)
  • Lightarm circles — forward and backward, 30 sec each
  • Foam roll or gentle chest stretches on floor, 1 min
  • Bodyweight push-ups × 10 (slow, controlled tempo)
  • Light DB floor press × 8 with 5 lb — blood flow activation
  • Shoulder blade squeezes × 10 — rear delt activation
🏋️ Workout
CHEST (5 exercises — priority focus)
DB Floor Press
Lie flat on your back on the floor and press the dumbbells up from chest level until your arms are straight.
4 sets 8–10 reps
Primary compound — go heavy and controlled. Pause briefly at the chest.
Weight (lb):
Wide Push-Up
Standard push-up with hands placed wider than shoulder-width, lowering your chest to the floor.
3 sets 12–15 reps Bodyweight
Hands slightly wider than shoulders. Full range of motion — chest taps floor if possible.
Incline Push-Up (feet elevated on couch)
Push-up with feet raised on a couch or sturdy elevated surface, hands on the floor.
3 sets 10–15 reps Bodyweight
Feet elevated 12–18 inches. Emphasizes upper chest. Keep core tight and hips flat.
DB Floor Fly
Lie on your back on the floor with arms extended above chest in a slight arc, then lower the dumbbells out to the sides with a slight bend in the elbows and return.
3 sets 10–12 reps
Controlled descent — feel the stretch at the chest at the bottom. Squeeze hard at the top.
Weight (lb):
Decline Push-Up (hands elevated on couch)
Push-up with hands on a couch or elevated surface and feet on the floor, targeting the lower chest.
3 sets 10–15 reps Bodyweight
Hands on couch edge, body in a straight line from head to heels. Drive through your hands.
TRICEPS (3 exercises)
DB Skull Crusher (floor)
Lie on your back on the floor and lower the dumbbells toward your temples by bending only at the elbows, then extend arms back up.
3 sets 10–12 reps
Keep elbows pointed toward the ceiling and tucked in — don't let them flare out.
Weight (lb):
DB Overhead Tricep Extension
Stand or sit and press a dumbbell straight overhead with both hands, then lower it slowly behind your head by bending at the elbows and return.
3 sets 12–15 reps
Keep upper arms stationary and close to your ears. Full stretch at the bottom.
Weight (lb):
Close-Grip Push-Up
Push-up with hands placed directly under your chest, elbows tracking close to your ribs as you lower and press.
3 sets 10–15 reps Bodyweight
Hands touching or slightly inside shoulder width. Elbows stay tight to your sides.
DB Kickback
Hinge at the hips with one arm supporting your weight, then extend the dumbbell straight back by contracting the tricep.
3 sets 12–15 reps
Full extension at the top — squeeze the tricep hard. Keep upper arm parallel to the floor throughout.
Weight (lb):
❄️ Cool-Down / Stretch (2 min)
  • Child's pose — hold 30 sec, breathe deep
  • Doorframe chest stretch — 30 sec per side, arms at 90°
  • Tricep stretch — overhead reach and gently pull elbow across head, 30 sec/side
  • Seated forward fold — stretch lats and chest, 30 sec
  • Deep breathing — 5 slow, full breaths to close the session
