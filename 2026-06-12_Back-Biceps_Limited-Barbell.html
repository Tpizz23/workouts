Back + Biceps

Limited-Barbell45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbProgressive overload focus

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Hip hinges with reach — 2 × 10
  • Scapular retractions — 2 × 12
  • Light rows/pulldowns — 2 ramp sets
  • Easy curls — 1 × 15 to warm elbows

Workout

Back

Chest-Supported DB Row on Couch

Lie chest-down on an incline couch setup and row without using momentum.

4 sets8–12

Base row anchor; squeeze shoulder blades hard.

DB Pullover Lat Focus

Use soft elbows and pull from the lats to move a dumbbell from behind the head to above the chest.

3 sets10–12

Keep elbows soft and pull from the lats.

DB Row with 2-Second Squeeze

Pause each row with elbows back to make the mid-back work harder.

3 sets10–12

Pause at the top of every rep.

Prone Y-T-W Raise

Bodyweight

Lie face-down and raise your arms into Y, T, and W shapes for rear-delt and upper-back strength.

3 sets8 each shape

Control each letter shape.

Biceps

DB Zottman Curl

Curl palms-up, rotate at the top, and lower palms-down for biceps plus forearms.

3 sets10–12

Curl up palms-up, lower palms-down.

Cross-Body Hammer Curl

Curl each dumbbell toward the opposite shoulder to emphasize brachialis and forearm strength.

3 sets10–12/side

Curl toward the opposite shoulder.

DB Drag Curl

Drag elbows backward as you curl to keep the bar path close to the torso.

3 sets10–12

Pull elbows back and keep reps strict.

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Child’s pose with side reach — 45 sec/side
  • Cross-body rear delt stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Forearm/biceps wall stretch — 30 sec/side

Progressive Overload Log

Log weight by set. When you hit the top of the rep range with clean form across all sets, add 5 lb next time.
