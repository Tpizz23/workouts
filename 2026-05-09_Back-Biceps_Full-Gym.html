💪 Workout

Back

Barbell Bent-Over Row
Use an overhand grip and pull the bar toward your lower ribs while keeping your torso braced.
4 sets8–10 repsWeight (lb)
Lat Pulldown (wide grip)
Pull the bar to upper chest with elbows driving down to train lats through a vertical pull.
3 sets10–12 repsWeight (lb)
Seated Cable Row (V-bar)
Drive elbows back and squeeze shoulder blades together on each controlled cable row.
3 sets10–12 repsWeight (lb)
Single-Arm DB Row
Brace on a bench and row heavy through a full range of motion one side at a time.
3 sets10–12/side repsWeight (lb)
Meadows Row
Use a landmine angle for a one-arm row that hits lats and upper back differently.
3 sets10/side repsWeight (lb)

Biceps

Incline DB Curl
Curl from an incline bench to stretch and load the long head of the biceps.
3 sets12 repsWeight (lb)
Cable Curl (straight bar)
Curl against constant cable tension while keeping elbows pinned by your sides.
3 sets12–15 repsWeight (lb)
Hammer Curl
Use a neutral grip for brachialis and brachioradialis thickness.
3 sets12–15 repsWeight (lb)
