Legs + Shoulders + Core

2026-05-19 · Tuesday at 9:00 AM

Limited-Barbell45–60 minBulk: 165 → 180 lbTuesday · 9:00 AMVariant B

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • ➜ 2 minutes brisk walk or marching in place
  • ➜ Hip circles x 10/side and ankle rocks x 10/side
  • ➜ Bodyweight good mornings x 12 plus squat-to-stand x 8
  • ➜ Light DB overhead press x 12 before working sets

Workout

Legs

DB Goblet Squat

A heavy dumbbell squat held at chest height to build quads and glutes.

4 sets10–12 reps

Deep squat, chest tall, controlled drive up.

DB Step-Up

A single-leg step onto a sturdy chair or bench substitute for quad and glute size.

3 sets10/leg reps

Drive through the whole foot; control the lowering.

DB Single-Leg RDL

A balance-focused hip hinge that loads hamstrings and glutes one side at a time.

3 sets8–10/leg reps

Keep hips square and reach the free leg long.

Wall Sit with DB

A weighted isometric hold that floods the quads without needing heavy equipment.

3 sets30–45 sec reps

Hold dumbbell goblet-style and keep knees tracking over toes.

Shoulders

Seated DB Shoulder Press

A seated dumbbell press that focuses shoulder strength while reducing leg drive.

4 sets8–10 reps

Press smoothly; stop just short of locking out aggressively.

Lean-Away DB Lateral Raise

A side-delt raise with a longer range of tension from a lean-away setup.

3 sets10–12/side reps

Lead with elbows and avoid shrugging.

Prone Rear Delt Y-Raise

A face-down raise pattern that trains rear delts and shoulder control.

3 sets12 reps

Move slowly and squeeze the upper back.

Core

DB Suitcase Carry/March

A one-sided loaded march that challenges the obliques to resist leaning.

3 sets30 sec/side reps

Stay tall; ribs down; slow steps.

Side Plank

Bodyweight

A bodyweight hold that builds oblique and anti-lateral-flexion strength.

3 sets30–45 sec/side reps

Stack shoulders and hips; breathe behind the brace.

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • ❄ Standing quad stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Hamstring fold with soft knees — 45 sec
  • ❄ Doorway pec/shoulder stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Child’s pose with reach — 45 sec

Progressive Overload Log

If you hit the top of the rep range with clean form on every set, add 5 lb next time.
