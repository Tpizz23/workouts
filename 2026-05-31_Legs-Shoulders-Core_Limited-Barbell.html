Legs + Shoulders + Core

Sunday · 2026-05-31 · Limited-Barbell

45–60 min workoutBulk focusProgressive overload

Warm-Up — 5 min

  • Bodyweight squats and reverse lunges — 2 × 10 each
  • Hip hinges plus glute bridges — 2 × 10
  • Shoulder circles and wall slides — 60 sec
  • One lighter ramp-up squat/press set before working weight

Workout

Legs

DB Front Squat

Hold two dumbbells at your shoulders and squat deep while keeping your torso tall.

4 sets8–12 repsQuad emphasis

DB Single-Leg RDL

Hinge on one leg with dumbbells while the back leg reaches behind for balance.

3 sets8–10/leg repsBalance hamstring/glute

DB Step-Up

Step onto a sturdy chair or bench substitute and stand tall without bouncing off the back leg.

3 sets10/leg repsControlled single-leg drive

Wall Sit with DB

Hold a goblet dumbbell while sitting against a wall with thighs near parallel.

3 sets30–45 sec repsQuad burn

Shoulders

Seated DB Shoulder Press

Sit tall and press dumbbells overhead without using leg drive.

4 sets8–10 repsStable press anchor

Lean-Away DB Lateral Raise

Lean away from a support and raise one dumbbell out to the side through a long range.

3 sets10–12/side repsLong side-delt tension

Prone Rear Delt Y-Raise

Lie face-down and raise your arms in a Y pattern to train rear delts and shoulder control.

3 sets12 repsRear delt control

Core

DB Suitcase Carry/March

Hold one heavy dumbbell at your side and march or walk slowly without leaning.

3 sets30 sec/side repsAnti-lean core

Hollow Body Hold

Hold a rounded-body position with shoulders and legs off the floor while bracing hard.

3 sets30 sec repsFull core tensionBodyweight

Cool-Down / Stretch — 2 min

  • Couch hip-flexor stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Hamstring fold — 45 sec/side
  • Cross-body shoulder stretch — 30 sec/side
  • Dead-bug breathing reset — 60 sec

Progressive Overload Log

Record working weight or reps. If all sets hit the top of the range cleanly, add 5 lb next time.
