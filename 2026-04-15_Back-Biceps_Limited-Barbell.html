Back + Biceps
Build size with purpose today — strong compounds first, then clean hypertrophy work to finish the target muscles.
Equipment: Limited-BarbellDuration: 45–60 minGoal: Bulk · Chest priorityStart: 10:30 AM
Warm-Up
  • Cat-cow, thoracic rotations, and hip hinges for 2 rounds to prep the posterior chain.
  • 1 light set of DB bent-over row and hammer curls before loading up.
  • Plank shoulder taps for 30 seconds to switch on bracing and scapular control.
Workout

Back

DB Bent-Over Row

Hinge at the hips and row both dumbbells toward the lower ribs while keeping the back flat.

4 sets8–10 reps

Hinge at hips, chest up

Single-Arm DB Row

Brace on a chair or couch and pull the dumbbell toward the hip for a full lat contraction.

3 sets10–12 reps

Brace on chair/couch

DB Renegade Row

From a plank, row one dumbbell at a time without letting the hips twist.

3 sets8–10 reps

Core stability, plank position

DB Romanian Deadlift

Push the hips back and lower the dumbbells along the thighs to load hamstrings and lower back.

3 sets10–12 reps

Hip hinge, hamstring stretch

Reverse Snow Angel (prone)

Lie face-down and sweep the arms in a wide arc to train rear delts and upper back control.

3 sets12 repsBodyweight

Rear delts and rhomboids

Biceps

DB Curl

Curl with palms turning up as you lift so the biceps fully shorten at the top.

3 sets10–12 reps

Supinate at top

DB Hammer Curl

Lift with a neutral grip to hit the brachialis and forearms alongside the biceps.

3 sets12–15 reps

Neutral grip, brachialis

DB Concentration Curl

Brace the elbow against the inner thigh and curl slowly for strict isolation.

3 sets12 reps

Elbow braced on inner thigh

Cool-Down / Stretch
  • Child’s pose with hands long for 45 seconds.
  • Cross-body rear delt stretch and hanging lat reach for 30 seconds per side.
  • Gentle biceps wall stretch for 30 seconds per side.
