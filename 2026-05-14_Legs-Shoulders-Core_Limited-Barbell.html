Workout

Legs

DB Front Squat

A two-dumbbell front squat that emphasizes quads and upright torso control.

4 sets8–12 reps

Quad-focused squat anchor.

DB Bulgarian Split Squat

A rear-foot-elevated split squat that loads each leg hard for size and stability.

3 sets8–10/leg reps

Single-leg strength and stability.

DB Hip Thrust on Couch

A couch-supported hip thrust that overloads glutes through a strong lockout.

3 sets12–15 reps

Glute lockout focus.

DB Single-Leg Calf Raise

A weighted single-leg calf raise for full stretch and strong top contraction.

3 sets15–20 reps

Calf stretch and squeeze.

Shoulders

Seated DB Shoulder Press

A seated press that reduces leg drive and keeps focus on the shoulders.

4 sets8–10 reps

Strict overhead strength.

Lean-Away DB Lateral Raise

A lateral raise with a lean that keeps tension on the side delts longer.

3 sets10–12/side reps

Long-tension side delts.

Prone Rear Delt Y-Raise

Bodyweight

A prone Y-shaped raise that builds rear delts and lower-trap control.

3 sets12 reps

Rear delt and lower-trap control.

Core

DB Suitcase Carry/March

A one-sided loaded march that builds obliques and grip while resisting side bend.

3 sets30 sec/side reps

Anti-side-bend core and grip.

Bear Crawl Hold

Bodyweight

A knees-hovering crawl position that builds deep core and shoulder stability.

3 sets30 sec reps

Deep brace and shoulder stability.
