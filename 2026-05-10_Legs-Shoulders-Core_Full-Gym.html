Legs

Leg Press

Leg Curl (lying or seated)
Curl heels toward glutes and control the lowering phase for hamstrings.
3 sets12 repsWeight (lb)
Leg Extension
Extend knees and squeeze quads hard at the top without swinging.
3 sets12–15 repsWeight (lb)
Standing Calf Raise (machine)
Use a full stretch at the bottom and a pause at the top for calves.
4 sets15–20 repsWeight (lb)
Walking DB Lunge
Move forward with controlled single-leg steps to hit quads and glutes.
3 sets10–12/leg repsWeight (lb)
Cable Pull-Through
Hinge against cable tension to train glutes and hamstrings without spinal loading.
3 sets12–15 repsWeight (lb)

Shoulders

Machine Shoulder Press
Press on a stable machine for controlled shoulder overload and a clean path.
3 sets10–12 repsWeight (lb)
Cable Lateral Raise
Raise one arm against cable tension to keep the side delt working the whole rep.
3 sets15 repsWeight (lb)
Face Pull to External Rotation
Pull to face level and rotate outward for rear delts and rotator cuff strength.
3 sets12–15 repsWeight (lb)

Core
