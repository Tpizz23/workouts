Chest + Triceps

Sunday · 2026-05-31 · Full-Gym

45–60 min workoutBulk focusProgressive overload

Warm-Up — 5 min

  • Arm circles and shoulder openers — 45 sec each direction
  • Scapular push-ups — 2 × 10
  • Light push-ups or empty-bar press pattern — 2 × 8
  • Two ramp-up sets on the first press before working weight

Workout

Chest

Barbell Bench Press

Press the bar from your chest to lockout on a bench with tight upper-back setup.

4 sets6–8 repsPrimary compound — heavy, progressive

Incline DB Press

Press dumbbells on an incline bench to emphasize upper chest.

3 sets8–10 repsUpper chest press

Cable Fly (low to high)

Sweep cable handles upward and inward to squeeze the upper chest.

3 sets12–15 repsUpper chest stretch and squeeze

Pec Deck Machine

Use the machine arms to bring elbows together and squeeze the chest.

3 sets12–15 repsStable peak contraction

Weighted Dips (chest lean)

Lean forward through dips to load chest while keeping shoulders controlled.

3 sets8–12 repsChest-leaning bonus movement

Triceps

Cable Tricep Pushdown (rope)

Press a rope down and flare the ends out to fully contract the triceps.

3 sets12–15 repsRope lockout focus

EZ-Bar Skull Crusher

Lower an EZ-bar toward your forehead and extend elbows without shoulder swing.

3 sets10–12 repsControlled elbow extension

Overhead Tricep Extension (cable)

Face away from the cable and extend overhead from a deep tricep stretch.

3 sets12–15 repsLong-head stretch

Cool-Down / Stretch — 2 min

  • Doorway pec stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Overhead tricep stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Slow nasal breathing with arms supported — 60 sec

Record working weight or reps. If all sets hit the top of the range cleanly, add 5 lb next time.
