Legs + Shoulders + Core

Limited-Barbell45–60 minBulk focus

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • → 2 min incline walk or bike
  • → Leg swings, hip circles, and bodyweight squats
  • → 1 light squat set and shoulder circles

Workout

Legs

DB Front Squat

Hold dumbbells at shoulder height, sit between your hips, and drive up through your feet for quad-focused leg strength.

4 sets8–12

DB Single-Leg RDL

Balance on one leg, hinge your hips back, and stand by squeezing the working hamstring and glute.

3 sets8–10/leg

DB Step-Up

Step onto a sturdy elevated surface, drive through the top foot, and control the descent.

3 sets10/leg

DB Hip Thrust on Couch

Rest your upper back on the couch and drive your hips upward, squeezing your glutes hard at the top.

3 sets12–15

DB Single-Leg Calf Raise

Rise slowly onto one foot while holding a dumbbell, then lower through a complete calf stretch.

3 sets15–20

Shoulders

DB Arnold Press

Start with palms facing you and rotate them outward as you press overhead to train the shoulders.

3 sets10–12

Lean-Away DB Lateral Raise

Hold support, lean away slightly, and raise the dumbbell to shoulder height for a longer side-delt range.

3 sets10–12/side

Prone Rear Delt Y-Raise

Lie face-down and lift your arms into a Y with controlled shoulders to target rear delts and stability.

3 sets12Bodyweight

Core

Dead Bug

Lie on your back and slowly extend opposite arm and leg while keeping your low back gently braced to the floor.

3 sets10/sideBodyweight

DB Suitcase Carry/March

Hold one heavy dumbbell at your side and walk or march without leaning to build oblique stability.

3 sets30 sec/side

Rest 2–3 min on the first compound movement; 60–90 sec on accessory work. Own each controlled rep.

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • ❄ Kneeling hip-flexor stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Hamstring stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Doorway shoulder stretch and slow nasal breathing — 30 sec

Progressive Overload Log

When every set reaches the top of its range with clean form, add 5 lb next time.
