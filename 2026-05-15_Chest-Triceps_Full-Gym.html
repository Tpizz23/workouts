Workout

Chest

Barbell Bench Press

A heavy barbell press that anchors chest strength and progressive overload.

4 sets6–8 reps

Heavy anchor: own the pause and drive hard.

Incline Cable Press

A cable press that keeps upper-chest tension consistent from stretch to lockout.

3 sets10–12 reps

Constant-tension upper chest press.

Decline Barbell/DB Press

A decline press that targets lower chest and lets you move solid load.

3 sets8–10 reps

Lower chest pressing volume.

Cable Fly (low to high)

A low-to-high cable fly that keeps constant tension on the upper chest.

3 sets12–15 reps

Upper-chest fly with full stretch.

Machine Chest Press

A guided chest press for controlled, high-quality pressing volume.

3 sets10–12 reps

Bonus controlled press without duplicating bench.

Triceps

Cable Tricep Pushdown (rope)

A rope pushdown that trains triceps lockout with elbow control.

3 sets12–15 reps

Lock out and spread the rope.

EZ-Bar Skull Crusher

A lying EZ-bar extension that isolates triceps through a controlled stretch.

3 sets10–12 reps

Strict elbow extension.

Single-Arm Cable Pushdown

A one-arm pushdown that targets lockout and balances triceps side to side.

3 sets12/side reps

Side-to-side triceps balance.

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • ❄ Doorway pec stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Overhead triceps stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Slow nasal breathing while shaking arms loose for 60 sec
