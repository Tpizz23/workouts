Chest + Triceps

Wednesday, 2026-07-08 · starts 10:30 AM

Full-Gym45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbRest: 60–90s isolation, 2–3m compounds

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Arm circles, bandless shoulder dislocates, and scapular push-ups for 90 seconds
  • Two ramp-up press sets before working weight
  • Light close-grip push-ups/extensions to warm elbows

Workout

Chest

Incline Cable Press

Press cable handles upward on an incline path for constant upper-chest tension.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Constant-tension upper chest press

Machine Chest Press

Press on a chest machine with stable control and a hard squeeze at lockout.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Controlled machine pressing variation

Pec Deck Machine

Bring the pec deck arms together while keeping your chest tall and shoulders down.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Squeeze hard at peak

Cable Crossover (high to low)

Sweep high cable handles down and together to target lower and inner chest.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: High-to-low cable path for lower/inner chest

Weighted Dips (chest lean)

Lean forward during dips to shift the load toward chest while still training pressing strength.

3 sets8–12 reps

Note: Lean forward to hit chest

Triceps

Reverse-Grip Cable Pushdown

Push a cable bar down with an underhand grip to emphasize triceps lockout.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Underhand cable pushdown for medial head

Overhead Tricep Extension (cable)

Face away from the cable and extend overhead from a deep triceps stretch.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Full stretch overhead

DB Tate Press

Lower dumbbells toward the chest with elbows out, then extend through the triceps.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Dumbbell tricep extension with elbows out

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Doorway pec stretch, 30 seconds per side
  • Overhead triceps stretch, 30 seconds per side
  • Slow nasal breathing with chest openers for 60 seconds

Progressive Overload Log

When all sets hit the top of the rep range with clean form, increase the load by 5 lb next time.
