Chest + Triceps

Thursday, 2026-07-02 · starts 9:00 AM

Limited-Barbell45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbRest: 60–90s isolation, 2–3m compounds

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Arm circles, bandless shoulder rolls, and scapular push-ups for 60 seconds
  • Two light ramp-up sets of your first press
  • 10 slow push-ups or wall presses to groove the pattern

Workout

Chest

DB Floor Press

Lie on the floor and press dumbbells from chest level until arms are locked out.

4 sets8–10 reps

Note: Primary compound — go heavy

Deficit Push-Up on DB Handles

Hold dumbbell handles as push-up grips to lower deeper and stretch the chest.

3 sets8–12 repsBodyweight

Note: Hands on dumbbells for a deeper chest stretch

DB Squeeze Press

Press two dumbbells together over your chest while squeezing inward the whole time.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Press dumbbells together to keep constant pec tension

DB Floor Fly with Pause

Open the dumbbells wide on the floor, pause in the stretch, then hug them back up.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Pause one second near the bottom before squeezing up

Triceps

DB Rolling Tricep Extension

Lower dumbbells beside your head, roll slightly back, then extend elbows hard.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Roll dumbbells back then extend for a bigger stretch

Single-Arm Overhead DB Extension

Hold one dumbbell overhead and bend only the elbow to train each tricep separately.

3 sets10–12/side reps

Note: One-arm overhead isolation for side-to-side balance

Diamond Push-Up

Do push-ups with hands close in a diamond shape to emphasize tricep lockout.

3 sets8–15 repsBodyweight

Note: Hands close together to bias triceps

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Doorway chest stretch — 30 seconds/side
  • Overhead tricep stretch — 30 seconds/side
  • Slow nasal breathing while relaxing shoulders for 60 seconds

Progressive Overload Log

If you hit the top of the rep range with clean form for all sets, increase weight by 5 lb next time.
