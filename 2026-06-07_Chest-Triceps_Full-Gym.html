Chest + Triceps

Sunday · 2026-06-07 · Full-Gym

45–60 min workoutBulk focusProgressive overload

Warm-Up — 5 min

  • Arm circles and shoulder openers — 45 sec each direction
  • Scapular push-ups — 2 × 10
  • Light push-ups or empty-bar press pattern — 2 × 8
  • Two ramp-up sets on the first press before working weight

Workout

Chest

Barbell Bench Press

Press the bar from your chest to lockout on a bench with tight upper-back setup.

4 sets6–8 repsPrimary compound — heavy, progressive

Machine Incline Chest Press

Press through a guided incline machine path for stable upper-chest overload.

3 sets8–12 repsStable upper-chest overload

Cable Crossover (high to low)

Pull high cables down and inward to hit lower and inner chest.

3 sets12–15 repsLower and inner chest line

Decline Barbell/DB Press

Press from a decline angle to bias the lower chest.

3 sets8–10 repsLower chest angle

Incline Cable Press

Press cable handles on an incline angle for constant upper-chest tension.

3 sets10–12 repsConstant-tension bonus press

Triceps

Close-Grip Bench Press

Bench with a narrower grip and tucked elbows to emphasize triceps.

3 sets8–10 repsHeavy tricep compound

Single-Arm Cable Pushdown

Press one cable handle down at a time for balanced lockout work.

3 sets12/side repsOne-arm lockout focus

Reverse-Grip Cable Pushdown

Push the cable down with an underhand grip to hit the medial tricep head.

3 sets12–15 repsUnderhand medial-head emphasis

Cool-Down / Stretch — 2 min

  • Doorway pec stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Overhead tricep stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Slow nasal breathing with arms supported — 60 sec
