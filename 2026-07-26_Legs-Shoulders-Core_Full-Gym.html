Legs + Shoulders + Core

Full-Gym45–60 minBulk focus

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • → 2 min incline walk or bike
  • → Leg swings, hip circles, and bodyweight squats
  • → 1 light squat set and shoulder circles

Workout

Legs

Barbell Back Squat

Brace the bar across your upper back, sit down between your hips, and drive through your feet to stand.

4 sets6–8

Romanian Deadlift

Push your hips back with a soft knee bend and stand by squeezing your glutes and hamstrings.

3 sets10–12

Bulgarian Split Squat

With one foot elevated behind you, lower under control through the front leg and drive back up.

3 sets8–10/leg

Leg Press

Lower the sled under control, then press through your whole foot without locking out hard.

3 sets10–12

Standing Calf Raise (machine)

Rise onto the balls of your feet, pause at the top, and lower through a full calf stretch.

4 sets15–20

Shoulders

Machine Shoulder Press

Press the handles overhead with your ribs down and lower them slowly to train the delts.

3 sets10–12

Cable Lateral Raise

Raise the cable handle out to shoulder height with a soft elbow to isolate the side delts.

3 sets15

Cable Reverse Fly

Sweep the cable handles out and back with soft elbows to train the rear delts and upper back.

3 sets12–15

Core

Cable Crunch (kneeling)

Kneel facing the cable and curl your ribs toward your hips instead of pulling with your arms.

3 sets15

Pallof Press (cable)

Press the cable straight out from your chest while resisting rotation to train core stability.

3 sets12/side

Rest 2–3 min on the first compound movement; 60–90 sec on accessory work. Own each controlled rep.

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • ❄ Kneeling hip-flexor stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Hamstring stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Doorway shoulder stretch and slow nasal breathing — 30 sec

Progressive Overload Log

When every set reaches the top of its range with clean form, add 5 lb next time.
