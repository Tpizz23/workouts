🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)

  • → Cat-cow and thoracic rotations, 60 sec
  • → Hip-hinge drill with empty hands, 2×10

  • → Prone Y-T-W raises, 1 easy round

💪 Workout

Back

Chest-Supported DB Row on Couch
Lean chest-down on a couch or bench substitute and row without swinging.
4 sets 8–12 reps Weight (lb)
Single-Arm DB Row
Brace one hand on a chair or couch and pull the dumbbell toward your hip.
3 sets 10–12/side reps Weight (lb)
DB Pullover Lat Focus
Move the dumbbell from overhead toward chest while thinking about pulling with your lats.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
DB Row with 2-Second Squeeze
Pause with elbows back on each rep to make the mid-back work harder.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
Prone Y-T-W Raise
Lie face-down and raise your arms into Y, T, and W positions for upper-back control.
3 sets 8 each shape reps Bodyweight

Biceps

DB Zottman Curl
Curl palms-up, rotate at the top, and lower palms-down to hit biceps and forearms.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
Cross-Body Hammer Curl
Curl the dumbbell toward the opposite shoulder to emphasize brachialis.
3 sets 10–12/side reps Weight (lb)
DB Drag Curl
Pull elbows back as the dumbbells slide up your torso for a strict bicep squeeze.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)

❄ Cool-Down / Stretch (2 min)

  • ❄ Lat stretch on couch/chair, 45 sec/side
  • ❄ Forearm flexor stretch, 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Child pose with side reach, 45 sec/side
  • ❄ Slow breathing in prone position, 60 sec
