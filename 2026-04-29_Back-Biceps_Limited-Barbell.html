  • → Cat-cow spinal mobility (10 reps, flowing)
  • → Band pull-aparts (2×15, squeeze shoulder blades)
  • → Scapular wall slides (10 reps, arms tracking up)

💪 Workout

Back

DB Bent-Over Row
Hinge at the hips with a dumbbell in each hand, pull the weights up toward your hips by bending the elbows.
4 sets 8–10 reps Weight (lb)
Single-Arm DB Row
Place one hand and knee on a bench and row a heavy dumbbell up to your hip, full range of motion.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
DB Renegade Row
In a high plank position holding two dumbbells, row one weight to your hip while keeping the rest of your body stable.
3 sets 8–10 reps Weight (lb)
DB Romanian Deadlift
With dumbbells in front of your thighs, hinge at the hips and lower the weights along your legs, feeling a hamstring stretch.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
Superman Hold
Lie face down and simultaneously lift your arms, chest, and legs off the ground, holding the Superman position.
3 sets 12 reps Bodyweight
Reverse Snow Angel (prone)
Lie face down with arms at your sides, lift arms and shoulders off the ground while sweeping in a snow angel pattern.
3 sets 12 reps Bodyweight

Biceps

DB Curl
Stand with dumbbells at your sides and curl the weights up toward your shoulders, turning your palms upward at the top.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
DB Hammer Curl
Curl dumbbells up with a neutral grip (palms facing each other) — hits the brachialis and outer bicep.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)
DB Concentration Curl
Sit down, brace your elbow against your inner thigh, and curl the dumbbell up focusing on the bicep squeeze.
3 sets 12 reps Weight (lb)
DB Incline Curl
Lie back on an incline bench with arms hanging down, curl the weights up keeping the long head stretched.
2 sets 12 reps Weight (lb)
