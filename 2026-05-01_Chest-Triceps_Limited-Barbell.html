Chest + Triceps
Friday, 2026-05-01
🏠 Home (Limited-Barbell)🔁 C — pump/mechanical drop variation⏱ 45–60 min🎯 Goal: 165 → 180 lb

🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)

  • → Arm circles, 30 sec each direction
  • → Incline push-ups, 2×8
  • → Light DB squeeze press, 1×15
  • → Tricep extension warm-up, 1×12 light

💪 Workout

Chest

Tempo Push-Up
Lower for three seconds, pause near the bottom, then press up with control.
3 sets 8–12 reps Bodyweight
DB Squeeze Press
Crush the dumbbells together as you press to keep your pecs under constant tension.
4 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
DB Close-Grip Floor Press
Press from the floor with elbows tucked to hit chest and triceps differently.
3 sets 8–12 reps Weight (lb)
DB Pullover (floor)
Move one dumbbell from overhead to chest level for chest, serratus, and lat stretch.
3 sets 12 reps Weight (lb)
Decline Push-Up (hands elevated on couch)
Use a couch or bench for a lower-chest push-up angle and controlled reps.
3 sets 10–15 reps Bodyweight

Triceps

DB Rolling Tricep Extension
Roll dumbbells back beside your head, then extend hard through the triceps.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
Close-Grip Push-Up
Keep hands under shoulders and elbows tight to load the triceps.
3 sets 10–15 reps Bodyweight
DB Kickback with 2-Second Hold
Hinge forward, extend the arm fully, and hold the lockout for two seconds.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)

❄ Cool-Down / Stretch (2 min)

  • ❄ Doorway chest stretch, 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Overhead tricep stretch, 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Wrist and forearm stretch, 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Slow nasal breathing lying on back, 60 sec
