Chest + Triceps

Sunday · 2026-05-31 · Limited-Barbell

45–60 min workoutBulk focusProgressive overload

Warm-Up — 5 min

  • Arm circles and shoulder openers — 45 sec each direction
  • Scapular push-ups — 2 × 10
  • Light push-ups or empty-bar press pattern — 2 × 8
  • Two ramp-up sets on the first press before working weight

Workout

Chest

DB Floor Press

Lie on the floor and press dumbbells from chest level to locked-out arms while keeping control.

4 sets8–10 repsPrimary compound — go heavy

Wide Push-Up

Set your hands wider than shoulder width and lower your chest under control before pressing up.

3 sets12–15 repsWide hand placement for pec volumeBodyweight

DB Floor Fly

Open the dumbbells wide on the floor, then squeeze your chest to bring them back above you.

3 sets10–12 repsControlled stretch at bottom

Incline Push-Up (feet elevated)

Place your feet up on a couch or chair to shift more work into the upper chest.

3 sets10–15 repsUpper chest bodyweight angleBodyweight

DB Pullover (floor)

Reach one dumbbell behind your head and pull it back over your chest with a controlled stretch.

3 sets12 repsChest and serratus stretch finisher

Triceps

DB Skull Crusher (floor)

Lie down and bend only your elbows to lower dumbbells beside your head before extending.

3 sets10–12 repsLight-moderate weight, elbows in

DB Overhead Tricep Extension

Hold a dumbbell overhead and bend your elbows to stretch and extend the triceps.

3 sets12–15 repsDeep stretch overhead

Close-Grip Push-Up

Keep your hands under your shoulders and elbows tight to bias the triceps.

3 sets10–15 repsTight-elbow tricep finisherBodyweight

Cool-Down / Stretch — 2 min

  • Doorway pec stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Overhead tricep stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Slow nasal breathing with arms supported — 60 sec
