💪 Chest + Triceps

Tuesday, April 7, 2026  ·  Full-Gym
🏋️ Full-Gym ⏱ 45–60 min 🎯 Bulk: 165 → 180 lb ⚖️ Priority: Chest
🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)
  • Lightarm circles — forward and backward, 30 sec each direction
  • Dynamic chest stretches in doorframe — 1 min total
  • Light barbell bench press with just the bar × 8 reps
  • Cable fly warm-up set with very light weight × 10
  • Shoulder blade squeezes and band pull-aparts × 10
🏋️ Workout
CHEST (5 exercises — priority focus)
Barbell Bench Press
Lie flat on a bench, lower the barbell to your mid-chest, then press it back up to full arm extension.
4 sets 6–8 reps
Primary compound — go heavy with 2–3 min rest between sets. Arch your back slightly and drive your feet into the floor.
Weight (lb):
Incline DB Press
Set an adjustable bench to 30–45 degrees and press dumbbells from chest level up to full extension.
3 sets 8–10 reps
Upper chest emphasis. Keep a slight inward angle on the dumbbells at the top to squeeze the inner chest.
Weight (lb):
Cable Fly (low to high)
Stand between two cable stations set low, grips in each hand, and bring your arms together in an arc in front of your chest.
3 sets 12–15 reps
Full stretch at the bottom and a hard squeeze at the top. Lean slightly forward to increase the arc.
Decline Barbell Press
Secure your legs at the top of a decline bench and press a barbell from your lower chest down to full extension.
3 sets 8–10 reps
Lower chest emphasis. Keep a controlled descent — 2 seconds down, 1 second pause, drive up.
Weight (lb):
Weighted Dips (chest lean)
Using dip bars or a bench, lean your torso forward slightly and lower yourself down, then press back up.
3 sets 8–12 reps
Lean forward about 30° to shift emphasis to chest. Add weight with a dip belt if available.
Weight (lb):
TRICEPS (4 exercises)
Cable Tricep Pushdown (rope)
With a rope attachment on a high cable, push down by extending at the elbows until arms are fully straight, then return.
3 sets 12–15 reps
Keep elbows pinned to your sides. At the bottom, flair the rope ends outward to fully activate the triceps.
EZ-Bar Skull Crusher
Lie on a flat bench and lower an EZ-bar toward your temples by bending only at the elbows, then extend back to full arm length.
3 sets 10–12 reps
The angled grip is easier on the wrists. Control the negative — 2 seconds down, pause, squeeze up.
Weight (lb):
Overhead Tricep Extension (cable)
With a rope or single-handle on a high cable, pull the handle down behind your head by extending at the elbows, then return.
3 sets 12–15 reps
Keep upper arms perpendicular to the floor. Full stretch overhead and a hard contraction at the bottom.
Close-Grip Bench Press
Lie on a flat bench with hands placed close together on the bar (6–8 inches apart) and press straight up, lowering to the chest.
3 sets 8–10 reps
Elbows stay tight to your sides — this keeps triceps fully engaged. Use a spotter for heavier sets.
Weight (lb):
❄️ Cool-Down / Stretch (2 min)
  • Chest stretch in doorway — arms at 90°, lean through for 30 sec per side
  • Pec deck stretch — hold the open position for 30 sec
  • Overhead tricep stretch — gently pull elbow across forehead, 30 sec/side
  • Foam roll chest — slow roll each side for 1 min
  • 5 deep breaths — full exhale to close the session
