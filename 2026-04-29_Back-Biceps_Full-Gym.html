Back + Biceps
Wednesday, 2026-04-29
🏋️ Full Gym ⏱ 45–60 min 🎯 Goal: 165 → 180 lb

🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)

  • → Cat-cow spinal mobility (10 reps)
  • → Band pull-aparts (2×15)
  • → Scapular wall slides (10 reps)
  • → Light pull-ups or Australian rows (1×12)

💪 Workout

Back

Pull-Up or Weighted Pull-Up
Hang from a bar with an overhand grip and pull yourself up until your chin clears the bar.
4 sets 6–10 reps Weight (lb)
Barbell Bent-Over Row
Hinge forward at the hips and pull a barbell up to your lower ribs, squeezing your shoulder blades.
4 sets 8–10 reps Weight (lb)
Seated Cable Row (V-bar)
Sit at a cable row station and pull the handle to your lower chest, driving your elbows back.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
Lat Pulldown (wide grip)
Sit at the lat pulldown machine and pull the bar down to your upper chest, squeezing the lats.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
Face Pull (cable, rope)
At a cable station, pull the rope attachment to your face while keeping elbows high and squeezing rear delts.
3 sets 15 reps Weight (lb)
Single-Arm DB Row
Place one hand and knee on a bench and row a heavy dumbbell up to your hip, full range of motion.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)

Biceps

Barbell Curl
Standing with a barbell, curl the weight up by bending only at the elbows, keeping your upper arms still.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
Incline DB Curl
Lie back on an incline bench and curl dumbbells up, feeling a stretch in the long head of the biceps.
3 sets 12 reps Weight (lb)
Cable Curl (straight bar)
At a low cable station, curl the bar upward while keeping your elbows stationary at your sides.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)
Hammer Curl
Curl dumbbells with a neutral grip, keeping palms facing each other throughout the movement.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)
Preacher Curl (EZ bar or machine)
Sit at a preacher bench and curl the weight up with no body english — pure isolation.
2 sets 12 reps Weight (lb)
