Back + Biceps

Purposeful hypertrophy session for TJ’s bulk — controlled reps, logged weights, and progressive overload.

Full-Gym45–60 min training targetBulk goal: 165 → 180 lbRotation index 1

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Hip hinges + thoracic rotations — 60 sec
  • Prone Y-T-W raises — 2×8 each
  • Light rows — 2×12
  • Slow bodyweight good mornings — 10 reps

Workout

Back

Pull-Up or Weighted Pull-Up

Pull from a dead hang until the chin clears the bar to build lats.

4 sets6–10 reps

Full dead hang to chin over bar

Barbell Bent-Over Row

Hinge over and row a barbell to the lower ribs for back thickness.

4 sets8–10 reps

Overhand, chest up, big pull

Seated Cable Row (V-bar)

Row a cable handle to the torso while squeezing the shoulder blades.

3 sets10–12 reps

Drive elbows back, squeeze

Lat Pulldown (wide grip)

Pull the bar to the upper chest with a wide grip to target the lats.

3 sets10–12 reps

When pull-ups are done

Face Pull (cable, rope)

Pull a rope toward the face with elbows high to train rear delts and shoulder health.

3 sets15 reps

External rotation, rear delts

Biceps

Barbell Curl

Curl a barbell with strict posture for heavy biceps loading.

3 sets10–12 reps

Standing, strict form

Incline DB Curl

Curl dumbbells on an incline bench to stretch and hit the long head.

3 sets12 reps

Long head stretch on incline bench

Cable Curl (straight bar)

Curl a cable bar to keep tension on the biceps through the full range.

3 sets12–15 reps

Constant tension

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Lat stretch on wall/chair — 45 sec/side
  • Cross-body rear-delt stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Forearm/biceps wall stretch — 30 sec/side

Progressive Overload Log

If all sets hit the top of the rep range with clean form, increase the load by 5 lb next time. Keep rest honest: 2–3 min compounds, 60–90 sec isolation.
