Legs + Shoulders + Core

Purposeful hypertrophy session for TJ’s bulk — controlled reps, logged weights, and progressive overload.

Full-Gym45–60 min training targetBulk goal: 165 → 180 lbRotation index 2

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Bodyweight squats — 2×12
  • Walking lunges — 10/leg
  • Shoulder circles + wall slides — 60 sec
  • Plank breathing/bracing — 30 sec

Workout

Legs

Barbell Back Squat

Squat a barbell on the upper back to build quads, glutes, and total lower-body strength.

4 sets6–8 reps

Primary compound — heavy

Leg Press

Press the sled through the feet for heavy leg volume with back support.

3 sets10–12 reps

High foot placement for glutes/hams

Romanian Deadlift

Hinge with a barbell or dumbbells to train hamstrings and glutes.

3 sets10–12 reps

Hip hinge, slight knee bend

Leg Curl (lying or seated)

Curl the pad toward the glutes to isolate hamstrings.

3 sets12 reps

Full extension, controlled

Standing Calf Raise (machine)

Raise the heels on a calf machine for direct calf work.

4 sets15–20 reps

Full stretch at bottom

Shoulders

Barbell or DB Overhead Press

Press weight overhead from shoulder height for shoulder strength.

4 sets8–10 reps

Seated or standing

Cable Lateral Raise

Raise one arm out to the side against cable tension for side delts.

3 sets15 reps

Constant cable tension

DB Rear Delt Fly

Sweep dumbbells outward from a hinged or chest-supported position to hit rear delts.

3 sets12–15 reps

Bent over or on incline bench face-down

Core

Cable Crunch (kneeling)

Kneel at a cable and crunch the ribs toward the pelvis against resistance.

3 sets15 reps

Crunch down, not pull

Hanging Leg Raise

Hang from a bar and raise the legs to target lower abs.

3 sets12–15 reps

Strict, no swing

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Couch quad stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Hamstring stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Figure-four glute stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Deep belly breathing in child’s pose — 60 sec

Progressive Overload Log

If all sets hit the top of the rep range with clean form, increase the load by 5 lb next time. Keep rest honest: 2–3 min compounds, 60–90 sec isolation.
