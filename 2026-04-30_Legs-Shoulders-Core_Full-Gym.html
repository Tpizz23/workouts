Legs + Shoulders + Core

🏋️ Full Gym🔁 B — unilateral legs and cable core variation⏱ 45–60 min🎯 Goal: 165 → 180 lb

🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)

  • → Bike or incline walk, 4 min
  • → Bodyweight squats, 2×10
  • → Hip airplanes or leg swings, 1×8/side
  • → Light shoulder press, 1×12

💪 Workout

Legs

Barbell Back Squat
Main heavy squat anchor; brace hard and keep depth consistent.
4 sets6–8 repsWeight (lb)
Romanian Deadlift
Hinge with a soft knee and feel hamstrings stretch without rounding.
3 sets8–10 repsWeight (lb)
Bulgarian Split Squat
Rear foot on bench for a quad/glute unilateral challenge.
3 sets8–10/leg repsWeight (lb)
Hack Squat Machine
Use a machine squat angle for quad emphasis and controlled depth.
3 sets8–12 repsWeight (lb)
Barbell Hip Thrust
Drive hips up and pause at lockout for glute overload.
3 sets10–12 repsWeight (lb)
Seated Calf Raise
Bent-knee calf work to hit the soleus differently than standing raises.
4 sets12–15 repsWeight (lb)

Shoulders

Barbell or DB Overhead Press
Primary overhead press; keep ribs down and press in a straight path.
4 sets8–10 repsWeight (lb)
Machine Lateral Raise
Stable machine side-delt work with a strong top squeeze.
3 sets12–15 repsWeight (lb)
Cable Reverse Fly
Use cables for constant rear-delt tension across the full rep.
3 sets12–15 repsWeight (lb)
DB Arnold Press
Rotate through the press for front/side-delt volume.
3 sets10–12 repsWeight (lb)

Core

Cable Crunch (kneeling)
Crunch ribs toward pelvis without turning it into a pulldown.
3 sets15 repsWeight (lb)
Pallof Press (cable)
Press cable straight out and resist rotation through your trunk.
3 sets12/side repsWeight (lb)
Ab Wheel Rollout
Roll out only as far as you can maintain a braced spine.
3 sets8–12 repsBodyweight
Cable Woodchopper
Rotate through the torso with a controlled cable path.
3 sets12/side repsWeight (lb)
