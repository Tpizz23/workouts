Legs + Shoulders + Core
Thursday, 2026-04-30
🏋️ Full Gym ⏱ 45–60 min 🎯 Goal: 165 → 180 lb

🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)

  • → Bodyweight squats (2×15)
  • → Leg swings (10/leg, three directions)
  • → Light barbell front rack mobility (10 reps)
  • → Plank hold (1×30 sec)

💪 Workout

Legs

Barbell Back Squat
With a barbell on your upper back, squat down until thighs are at least parallel to the ground.
4 sets 6–8 reps Weight (lb)
Leg Press
Sit in the leg press machine and lower the platform by bending your knees, then press back up.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
Romanian Deadlift
With a barbell or dumbbells, hinge at the hips keeping a slight knee bend, lowering the weight down the legs.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
Leg Curl (lying or seated)
On the leg curl machine, curl the weight up by bending your knees, squeezing at the top.
3 sets 12 reps Weight (lb)
Leg Extension
On the leg extension machine, extend your legs until they are straight, squeezing the quads hard.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)
Barbell Hip Thrust
With your upper back on a bench and a barbell over your hips, drive your hips up squeezing glutes.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)
Standing Calf Raise (machine)
On the calf raise machine, rise up on your toes as high as possible and lower with a full stretch.
4 sets 15–20 reps Weight (lb)
Bulgarian Split Squat
With your rear foot on a bench, lower into a lunge on your front leg, then drive back up.
3 sets 8–10/leg reps Weight (lb)

Shoulders

Barbell or DB Overhead Press
Press a barbell or dumbbells from shoulder height to full overhead extension.
4 sets 8–10 reps Weight (lb)
Cable Lateral Raise
Stand next to a cable machine and raise your arm out to the side against the cable's constant tension.
3 sets 15 reps Weight (lb)
DB Rear Delt Fly
Bent over with dumbbells hanging, raise arms out to the sides squeezing your rear delts.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)
Cable Front Raise
With a cable at a low setting, raise one arm in front of you to shoulder height.
3 sets 12 reps Weight (lb)
Machine Shoulder Press
Sit in the shoulder press machine and press the handles overhead, squeezing at the top.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
DB Arnold Press
Start with dumbbells at shoulder height with palms facing you, press up while rotating palms to face forward.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)

Core

Cable Crunch (kneeling)
Kneel at a cable station and crunch down, pulling the rope attachment down with your abs.
3 sets 15 reps Weight (lb)
Hanging Leg Raise
Hang from a pull-up bar and raise your legs in front of you until they are parallel to the ground.
3 sets 12–15 reps Bodyweight
Ab Machine Crunch
Sit in the ab crunch machine and crunch forward against the resistance, controlled throughout.
3 sets 15 reps Weight (lb)
Plank
Hold a rigid push-up position on your forearms, keeping your body in a straight line from head to heels.
3 sets 45–60 sec reps Bodyweight
Russian Twist (weight plate)
Sit with feet off the ground and rotate your torso, tapping a weight plate to each side.
3 sets 20 reps Weight (lb)
Dead Bug
On your back with arms extended to the ceiling, alternately extend opposite arm and leg while keeping your lower back pressed to the floor.
3 sets 10/side reps Bodyweight

❄ Cool-Down / Stretch (2 min)

  • ❄ Foam roll quads (30 sec/side)
  • ❄ Foam roll IT bands (30 sec/side)
  • ❄ Calf stretch against wall or machine (30 sec/side)
  • ❄ Seated spinal twist (30 sec/side)
