Legs + Shoulders + Core
Thursday, 2026-04-30
🏠 Home (Limited-Barbell)🔁 B — unilateral legs and delt variation⏱ 45–60 min🎯 Goal: 165 → 180 lb

🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)

  • → Bodyweight squats, 2×10
  • → Reverse lunges, 1×8/side
  • → Shoulder circles and arm swings, 60 sec
  • → Dead bug, 1×8/side

💪 Workout

Legs

DB Front Squat
Hold dumbbells at your shoulders and squat tall for quad-focused work.
4 sets 8–12 reps Weight (lb)
DB Step-Up
Step onto a sturdy surface under control and drive through the front foot.
3 sets 10/leg reps Weight (lb)
DB Single-Leg RDL
Hinge on one leg while keeping hips square to train hamstrings and balance.
3 sets 8–10/leg reps Weight (lb)
DB Hip Thrust on Couch
Rest upper back on couch, place dumbbell on hips, and drive hips up hard.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)
Wall Sit with DB
Hold a dumbbell goblet-style while maintaining a strong wall-sit position.
3 sets 30–45 sec reps Weight (lb)

Shoulders

Seated DB Shoulder Press
Press from a seated position to remove leg drive and isolate shoulders.
4 sets 8–10 reps Weight (lb)
Lean-Away DB Lateral Raise
Lean away from support and raise the dumbbell to lengthen side-delt tension.
3 sets 10–12/side reps Weight (lb)
Prone Rear Delt Y-Raise
Lie face-down and raise arms in a Y to train rear delts and lower traps.
3 sets 12 reps Weight (lb)

Core

DB Suitcase Carry/March
Hold one heavy dumbbell and march slowly while resisting side-bending.
3 sets 30 sec/side reps Weight (lb)
Side Plank
Hold a straight side plank and keep hips high.
3 sets 30–45 sec/side reps Bodyweight
Bear Crawl Hold
Hover knees just off the floor and brace hard while breathing.
3 sets 30 sec reps Bodyweight

❄ Cool-Down / Stretch (2 min)

  • ❄ Half-kneeling hip flexor stretch, 45 sec/side
  • ❄ Hamstring stretch, 45 sec/side
  • ❄ Cross-body shoulder stretch, 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Child pose, 60 sec
