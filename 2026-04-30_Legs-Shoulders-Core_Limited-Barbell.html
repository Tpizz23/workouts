Legs + Shoulders + Core

Purposeful hypertrophy session for TJ’s bulk — controlled reps, logged weights, and progressive overload.

Limited-Barbell45–60 min training targetBulk goal: 165 → 180 lbRotation index 2

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Bodyweight squats — 2×12
  • Walking lunges — 10/leg
  • Shoulder circles + wall slides — 60 sec
  • Plank breathing/bracing — 30 sec

Workout

Legs

DB Goblet Squat

Hold one dumbbell at the chest and squat deep while keeping the torso tall.

4 sets10–12 reps

Deep squat, chest up

DB Bulgarian Split Squat

Elevate the rear foot and squat on one leg for quads, glutes, and balance.

3 sets8–10/leg reps

Rear foot elevated on chair

DB Romanian Deadlift

Hinge with dumbbells to load hamstrings and glutes while keeping a neutral spine.

3 sets10–12 reps

Hip hinge pattern

DB Reverse Lunge

Step backward into a lunge to train each leg with less knee stress.

3 sets10–12/leg reps

Controlled, knee tracks over toe

DB Single-Leg Calf Raise

Raise onto one toe while holding a dumbbell to isolate the calf.

3 sets15–20 reps

Hold DB for resistance

Shoulders

DB Overhead Press

Press dumbbells from shoulder level overhead for overall shoulder strength.

4 sets8–10 reps

Seated or standing

DB Lateral Raise

Raise dumbbells to the sides to build the side delts and shoulder width.

3 sets12–15 reps

Slight forward lean, pinky up

DB Rear Delt Fly

Hinge forward and sweep dumbbells outward to target the rear delts.

3 sets12–15 reps

Bent over, elbows slightly bent

Core

Plank

Bodyweight

Hold a rigid forearm plank to train anti-extension core strength.

3 sets45–60 sec reps

Brace hard

DB Russian Twist

Rotate a dumbbell side to side to challenge the obliques.

3 sets20 reps (10/side) reps

Feet off ground

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Couch quad stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Hamstring stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Figure-four glute stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Deep belly breathing in child’s pose — 60 sec

Progressive Overload Log

If all sets hit the top of the rep range with clean form, increase the load by 5 lb next time. Keep rest honest: 2–3 min compounds, 60–90 sec isolation.
