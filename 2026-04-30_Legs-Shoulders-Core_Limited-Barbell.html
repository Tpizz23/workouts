Legs + Shoulders + Core
Thursday, 2026-04-30
🏠 Home (Limited-Barbell) ⏱ 45–60 min 🎯 Goal: 165 → 180 lb

🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)

  • → Bodyweight squats (2×15, full depth)
  • → Leg swings (10/leg, front and side)
  • → Shoulder circles (30 sec each direction)
  • → Plank hold (1×30 sec, brace hard)

💪 Workout

Legs

DB Goblet Squat
Hold a dumbbell vertically at your chest and squat deep, keeping your torso upright and knees out.
4 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
DB Bulgarian Split Squat
Rear foot elevated on a chair or bench, lower into a lunge on the front leg.
3 sets 8–10/leg reps Weight (lb)
DB Romanian Deadlift
With dumbbells in front of your thighs, hinge at the hips and lower the weights along your legs, feeling a hamstring stretch.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
DB Reverse Lunge
Step backward into a lunge, lowering the back knee toward the ground, then push back up.
3 sets 10–12/leg reps Weight (lb)
DB Sumo Squat
Wide stance squat with toes pointed out, lowering your hips until thighs are parallel to the ground.
3 sets 12 reps Weight (lb)
DB Single-Leg Calf Raise
Standing on one leg on a step, raise up on your toes as high as possible, then lower with a full stretch.
3 sets 15–20 reps Weight (lb)
Glute Bridge with DB on hips
Lie on your back with a dumbbell resting on your hip crease and drive through your heel to raise your hips.
3 sets 15 reps Weight (lb)
Jump Squat (bodyweight)
Squat down and explode upward into a jump, landing softly and immediately going into the next rep.
3 sets 10 reps Bodyweight

Shoulders

DB Overhead Press
Press dumbbells up from shoulder height overhead, either seated or standing with a braced core.
4 sets 8–10 reps Weight (lb)
DB Lateral Raise
With a slight forward lean, raise dumbbells out to the sides until arms are parallel to the floor.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)
DB Front Raise
Standing tall, lift one dumbbell at a time in front of you to shoulder height, alternating arms.
3 sets 12 reps Weight (lb)
DB Arnold Press
Start with dumbbells at shoulder height with palms facing you, press up while rotating palms to face forward.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
DB Rear Delt Fly
Bent over with dumbbells hanging, raise arms out to the sides squeezing your rear delts.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)
DB Upright Row
Pull dumbbells straight up along your body to chin height, letting elbows flare out to the sides.
3 sets 12 reps Weight (lb)

Core

Plank
Hold a rigid push-up position on your forearms, keeping your body in a straight line from head to heels.
3 sets 45–60 sec reps Bodyweight
DB Russian Twist
Sit with feet off the ground and twist your torso side to side, tapping the dumbbell on each side.
3 sets 20 reps (10/side) reps Weight (lb)
Leg Raise (lying)
Lie flat on your back and raise your legs up until they are perpendicular to the floor, then lower slowly.
3 sets 12–15 reps Bodyweight
Dead Bug
On your back with arms extended to the ceiling, alternately extend opposite arm and leg while keeping your lower back pressed to the floor.
3 sets 10/side reps Bodyweight
Mountain Climbers
In a high plank position, rapidly drive your knees toward your chest one at a time.
3 sets 30 sec reps Bodyweight
DB Woodchopper
Stand with feet wide, rotate your torso and bring the dumbbell diagonally across your body in a chopping motion.
3 sets 12/side reps Weight (lb)
Hollow Body Hold
Lie on your back, raise arms and legs slightly off the ground, and hold a hollow, braced position.
3 sets 30 sec reps Bodyweight

❄ Cool-Down / Stretch (2 min)

  • ❄ Foam roll quads (30 sec/side)
  • ❄ Foam roll IT band (30 sec/side)
  • ❄ Calf stretch against a wall (30 sec/side)
  • ❄ Seated spinal twist (30 sec/side)
