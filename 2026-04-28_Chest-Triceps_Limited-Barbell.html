Chest + Triceps
Tuesday, 2026-04-28
🏠 Home (Limited-Barbell)🔁 B — unilateral/tempo variation⏱ 45–60 min🎯 Goal: 165 → 180 lb

🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)

  • → Arm circles, 30 sec each direction
  • → Scapular push-ups, 2×10
  • → Light DB floor press, 1×12
  • → Slow tempo push-ups, 1×6 with a 3-sec lower

💪 Workout

Chest

DB Floor Press
Lie on your back and press dumbbells from chest level; keep this as the heavy baseline press.
4 sets 8–10 reps Weight (lb)
Single-Arm DB Floor Press
Press one dumbbell at a time while bracing your core to prevent rotating.
3 sets 8–10/side reps Weight (lb)
DB Floor Fly with Pause
Lower with soft elbows, pause near the floor, then squeeze your chest to return.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
Deficit Push-Up on DB Handles
Hold dumbbells as handles so your chest can travel slightly deeper than a normal push-up.
3 sets 8–12 reps Bodyweight
DB Squeeze Press
Press two dumbbells together the whole time to keep constant pec tension.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)

Triceps

DB Tate Press
Lie on the floor with elbows out and press the dumbbells by extending your triceps.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
Single-Arm Overhead DB Extension
Work one arm overhead at a time for a deep tricep stretch and balanced control.
3 sets 10–12/side reps Weight (lb)
Diamond Push-Up
Place hands close together and keep elbows tucked to bias the triceps.
3 sets 8–15 reps Bodyweight

❄ Cool-Down / Stretch (2 min)

  • ❄ Doorway chest stretch, 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Overhead tricep stretch, 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Cross-body shoulder stretch, 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Child pose with long reaches, 60 sec
