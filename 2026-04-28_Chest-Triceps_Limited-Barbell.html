Chest + Triceps
Tuesday, 2026-04-28
🏠 Home (Limited-Barbell) ⏱ 45–60 min 🎯 Goal: 165 → 180 lb

🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)

  • → Arm circles (30 sec forward, 30 sec backward)
  • → Push-up variations: wide, narrow, incline (2×8 each)
  • → Light DB floor presses (1×12 at ~40% working weight)
  • → Shoulder dislocates with a broomstick or towel (10 reps)

💪 Workout

Chest

DB Floor Press
Lie on your back on the floor and press dumbbells up from your chest — like a bench press but on the ground.
4 sets 8–10 reps Weight (lb)
Wide Push-Up
Standard push-up with hands wider than shoulder-width, squeezing chest at the top.
3 sets 12–15 reps Bodyweight
DB Floor Fly
Lie on the floor with dumbbells extended above chest, lower with a slight bend in elbows, then squeeze back up.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
Incline Push-Up (feet elevated)
Place feet on an elevated surface and hands on the floor, lowering chest toward the ground.
3 sets 10–15 reps Bodyweight
DB Pullover (floor)
Lie perpendicular on a bench or couch with one end of a dumbbell in both hands, press up and overhead.
3 sets 12 reps Weight (lb)
Decline Push-Up (hands elevated on couch)
With hands on a couch or bench and feet on the floor, lower chest toward the elevated surface.
3 sets 10–15 reps Bodyweight
DB Floor Press
Lie on your back on the floor and press dumbbells up from your chest — like a bench press but on the ground.
4 sets 8–10 reps Weight (lb)

Triceps

DB Skull Crusher (floor)
Lie on the floor and lower a dumbbell toward your forehead by bending only your elbows, then press back up.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
DB Overhead Tricep Extension
Stand or sit with a dumbbell held overhead, lower it behind your head by bending elbows, then press back up.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)
Close-Grip Push-Up
Push-up with hands directly under your shoulders, keeping elbows tucked close to your sides.
3 sets 10–15 reps Bodyweight
DB Kickback
Hinge at the hip with one arm supporting your body, then extend the dumbbell behind you by straightening the elbow.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)

❄ Cool-Down / Stretch (2 min)

  • ❄ Foam roll or squeeze chest (30 sec/side)
  • ❄ Doorway stretch for chest (30 sec/side)
  • ❄ Overhead tricep stretch (30 sec/side)
  • ❄ Child's pose with arms extended (60 sec)
