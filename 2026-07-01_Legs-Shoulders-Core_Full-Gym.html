Legs + Shoulders + Core

Wednesday, 2026-07-01 · starts 12:00 PM

Full-Gym45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbRest: 60–90s isolation, 2–3m compounds

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Bodyweight squats, lunges, and hip circles for 90 seconds
  • Shoulder CARs and light lateral raises before pressing
  • Two ramp-up sets on the first leg movement

Workout

Legs

Barbell Back Squat

Squat with a barbell on your upper back, staying braced through the full range.

4 sets6–8 reps

Note: Primary compound — heavy

Hack Squat Machine

Use the hack squat sled to drive through the quads with a stable torso.

3 sets8–12 reps

Note: Quad-focused squat machine variation

Romanian Deadlift

Hinge with a barbell or dumbbells to stretch and load hamstrings and glutes.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Hip hinge, slight knee bend

Seated Calf Raise

Raise and lower the seated calf machine slowly for a full calf stretch.

4 sets12–15 reps

Note: Bent-knee calf emphasis

Shoulders

Barbell or DB Overhead Press

Press a barbell or dumbbells overhead while keeping ribs down and core braced.

4 sets8–10 reps

Note: Seated or standing

Machine Lateral Raise

Use the lateral raise machine to isolate the side delts through a controlled arc.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Stable side-delt pump work

Face Pull to External Rotation

Pull the rope toward your face and rotate slightly to hit rear delts and cuffs.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Rear delt and rotator cuff emphasis

Core

Cable Crunch (kneeling)

Kneel at a high cable and curl your ribs toward your hips with abs doing the work.

3 sets15 reps

Note: Crunch down, not pull

Pallof Press (cable)

Press a cable straight out from your chest while resisting torso rotation.

3 sets12/side reps

Note: Anti-rotation core stability

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Standing quad and hamstring stretches — 30 seconds/side
  • Figure-four glute stretch — 30 seconds/side
  • Doorway shoulder stretch and slow breathing

Progressive Overload Log

If you hit the top of the rep range with clean form for all sets, increase weight by 5 lb next time.
