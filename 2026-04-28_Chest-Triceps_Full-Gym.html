Chest + Triceps

Purposeful hypertrophy session for TJ’s bulk — controlled reps, logged weights, and progressive overload.

Full-Gym45–60 min training targetBulk goal: 165 → 180 lbRotation index 0

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Arm circles + bandless shoulder rotations — 60 sec
  • Scapular push-ups — 2×10
  • Light push-up ramp set — 2×8
  • Empty-hand press patterning and elbow warm-up — 60 sec

Workout

Chest

Barbell Bench Press

Press a barbell from the chest to build maximal pressing strength and pec mass.

4 sets6–8 reps

Primary compound — heavy, progressive

Incline DB Press

Press dumbbells on an incline bench to emphasize upper chest.

3 sets8–10 reps

Upper chest, 30–45° incline

Cable Fly (low to high)

Sweep cable handles upward and inward to squeeze the upper chest.

3 sets12–15 reps

Full stretch and contraction

Pec Deck Machine

Bring machine arms together to isolate the pecs with stable tension.

3 sets12–15 reps

Squeeze hard at peak

Decline Barbell/DB Press

Press from a decline angle to target the lower chest.

3 sets8–10 reps

Lower chest emphasis

Triceps

Cable Tricep Pushdown (rope)

Push a rope attachment down and apart to fully contract the triceps.

3 sets12–15 reps

Flare elbows out at bottom

EZ-Bar Skull Crusher

Lower an EZ-bar toward the forehead and extend the elbows to isolate triceps.

3 sets10–12 reps

Controlled descent

Overhead Tricep Extension (cable)

Face away from a cable and extend overhead to train the long head.

3 sets12–15 reps

Full stretch overhead

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Doorway pec stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Overhead triceps stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Child’s pose with long exhales — 60 sec

Progressive Overload Log

If all sets hit the top of the rep range with clean form, increase the load by 5 lb next time. Keep rest honest: 2–3 min compounds, 60–90 sec isolation.
