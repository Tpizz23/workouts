Chest

Primary heavy press for progressive overload; keep shoulder blades pinned and drive through the floor.

Machine Incline Chest Press
Use the machine to load upper chest with a stable path and controlled tempo.
3 sets8–12 repsWeight (lb)
Cable Crossover (high to low)

3 sets12–15 repsWeight (lb)
Weighted Dips (chest lean)

DB Squeeze Press

3 sets10–12 repsWeight (lb)

Triceps
