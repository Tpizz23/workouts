Chest + Triceps
Tuesday, 2026-04-28
🏋️ Full Gym ⏱ 45–60 min 🎯 Goal: 165 → 180 lb

🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)

  • → Arm circles (30 sec forward, 30 sec backward)
  • → Light barbell bench press (1×12 at ~40%)
  • → Incline dumbbell press (1×12 at ~40%)
  • → Shoulder dislocates with a bar (10 reps)

💪 Workout

Chest

Barbell Bench Press
Lie on a flat bench and press a barbell from chest level up to full arm extension.
4 sets 6–8 reps Weight (lb)
Incline DB Press
On an incline bench set to 30–45 degrees, press dumbbells from chest level up overhead.
3 sets 8–10 reps Weight (lb)
Cable Fly (low to high)
Stand in front of cable towers and bring the handles up and together in front of your chest, squeezing.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)
Pec Deck Machine
Sit in the pec deck machine and squeeze your arms together in front of your chest, controlled throughout.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)
Decline Barbell/DB Press
On a decline bench, press the barbell or dumbbells downward toward your lower chest.
3 sets 8–10 reps Weight (lb)
Weighted Dips (chest lean)
On dip bars with a forward lean, lower your body and press back up, targeting the chest.
3 sets 8–12 reps Weight (lb)
Barbell Bench Press
Lie on a flat bench and press a barbell from chest level up to full arm extension.
4 sets 6–8 reps Weight (lb)

Triceps

Cable Tricep Pushdown (rope)
At a cable station, push the rope attachment down by bending only your elbows, keeping them tucked.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)
EZ-Bar Skull Crusher
Lying on a bench, lower an EZ bar or barbell toward your forehead by bending the elbows, then press back up.
3 sets 10–12 reps Weight (lb)
Overhead Tricep Extension (cable)
With a rope or single-handle attached to a low cable, pull overhead and extend the arm upward.
3 sets 12–15 reps Weight (lb)
Close-Grip Bench Press
On a flat bench, press a barbell with hands shoulder-width apart, keeping elbows tight to your sides.
3 sets 8–10 reps Weight (lb)

❄ Cool-Down / Stretch (2 min)

  • ❄ Foam roll pecs (30 sec/side)
  • ❄ Doorway chest stretch (30 sec/side)
  • ❄ Overhead tricep stretch with rack or bar (30 sec/side)
  • ❄ Child's pose (60 sec)
