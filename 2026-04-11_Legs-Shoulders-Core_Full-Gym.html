🦵 Legs + Shoulders + Core

Saturday, April 11, 2026  ·  Full-Gym
🏋️ Full-Gym ⏱ 45–60 min 🎯 Bulk: 165 → 180 lb
🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)
  • Bodyweight squat and dynamic leg swings — 1 min total, legs and hips warm
  • Leg extension machine with light weight × 10 — quad activation
  • Leg curl machine with light weight × 8 — hamstring activation
  • Empty barbell back squat × 8 — spinal Erector and leg warm-up
  • Seated dumbbell shoulder press with very light weight × 8 — shoulder activation
🏋️ Workout
LEGS (4 exercises)
Barbell Back Squat
With a barbell on your upper back, squat down by breaking at the hips and knees simultaneously, keeping your chest up, until your hip crease is below the top of your knee, then drive up.
4 sets 6–8 reps
Primary compound for legs — go heavy with 2–3 min rest. Keep your core braced and breathe into your belly before each rep. Depth is non-negotiable for muscle growth.
Weight (lb):
Leg Press
Sit in the leg press machine with feet placed high and wide on the platform, lower the weight by bending your knees toward your chest, then press through your heels to extend.
3 sets 10–12 reps
High foot placement shifts emphasis to the glutes and hamstrings. Keep your lower back pressed into the pad and do not let your hips round at the bottom.
Weight (lb):
Romanian Deadlift
Stand holding a barbell at hip height, push your hips back while keeping your legs mostly straight, lower the bar along your shins, then drive your hips forward to return to standing.
3 sets 10–12 reps
Hip hinge pattern — not a squat. Keeps a slight knee bend but the work comes from the hamstrings and glutes. Feel a deep hamstring stretch at the bottom.
Weight (lb):
Standing Calf Raise (machine)
Stand on the calf raise machine with the pads resting on your shoulders, lower your heels below the platform for a full stretch, then rise up as high as possible onto your toes.
4 sets 15–20 reps
Full stretch at the bottom — drop the heel well below the platform. Pause for 1 second at the top and squeeze the calves hard. Straighten your legs fully between sets.
Weight (lb):
SHOULDERS (3 exercises)
Barbell Overhead Press
Stand with a barbell at shoulder height, brace your core, and press the bar straight overhead until your arms are fully extended, then lower with control.
4 sets 8–10 reps
Primary compound for shoulders. Keep your core tight and avoid excessive back arch. The bar should travel in a slight arc directly over your shoulder joints.
Weight (lb):
Cable Lateral Raise
Stand beside a cable tower with the low pulley set, and raise your arm out to the side in an arc until it reaches shoulder height, keeping a slight bend in the elbow.
3 sets 15 reps
Cable provides constant tension throughout the range — this hits the lateral deltoid at the most mechanically disadvantaged point. Slight forward lean improves the arc.
DB Rear Delt Fly
Bent over on an incline bench face-down (or standing with a slight hip hinge), raise the dumbbells out to the sides in a wide arc, squeezing your shoulder blades together at the top.
3 sets 12–15 reps
Targets the rear deltoids and upper back. The incline bench removes lower back from the equation. Lead with your elbows, not your hands, throughout the movement.
Weight (lb):
CORE (2 exercises)
Cable Crunch (kneeling)
Kneel in front of a high cable with a rope attachment, crunch down by flexing at the spine while pulling the rope down toward your knees, then slowly return.
3 sets 15 reps
Crunch down, not pull down — the movement comes from spinal flexion, not arm pulling. Keep your hips stationary and crunch with your abs, not your lats.
Hanging Leg Raise
Hang from a pull-up bar or captain's chair and raise your legs straight out in front of you until your body forms an L-shape, then lower with control.
3 sets 12–15 reps
Strict form — no swinging or kipping. If this is too hard, bend the knees slightly or start with knee raises. Full contraction in the lower abs at the top of each rep.
❄️ Cool-Down / Stretch (2 min)
  • Quad stretch on wall or rack — pull foot to glute, 30 sec/side
  • Seated hamstring stretch — extend one leg, reach for toes, 30 sec/side
  • Calf stretch on leg press — deep stretch for both gastroc and soleus, 30 sec/side
  • Lat stretch in doorframe — arms at 90°, lean through, 30 sec
  • 5 deep breaths — exhale fully to activate the parasympathetic system and close the session
