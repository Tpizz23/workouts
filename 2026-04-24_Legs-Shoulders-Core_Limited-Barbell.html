Legs + Shoulders + Core
Build size with purpose today — strong compounds first, then clean hypertrophy work to finish the target muscles.
Equipment: Limited-BarbellDuration: 45–60 minGoal: Bulk · Chest priorityStart: 3:30 PM
Warm-Up
  • Bodyweight squats, walking lunges, and arm circles for 2 rounds to raise temperature fast.
  • 1 light set of goblet squats and overhead presses before work sets.
  • Glute bridges for 15 reps plus dead bugs for 10 per side to turn on glutes and trunk.
Workout

Legs

DB Goblet Squat

Hold one dumbbell at chest height and sit down between the hips for a deep, upright squat.

4 sets10–12 reps

Deep squat, chest up

DB Bulgarian Split Squat

With the back foot elevated, lower under control and drive through the front leg.

3 sets8–10/leg reps

Rear foot elevated on chair

DB Romanian Deadlift

Push the hips back and keep a soft knee bend to stretch the hamstrings under load.

3 sets10–12 reps

Hip hinge pattern

DB Single-Leg Calf Raise

Rise onto one foot through a full range while holding a dumbbell for extra resistance.

3 sets15–20 reps

Hold DB for resistance

Shoulders

DB Overhead Press

Press the dumbbells overhead from shoulder level while keeping ribs stacked over hips.

4 sets8–10 reps

Seated or standing

DB Lateral Raise

Lift the dumbbells out to the sides until shoulder height without shrugging the traps.

3 sets12–15 reps

Slight forward lean, pinky up

DB Rear Delt Fly

Hinge forward and sweep the arms wide to target the rear shoulders.

3 sets12–15 reps

Bent over, elbows slightly bent

Core

Plank

Brace the abs and glutes hard while holding a straight line from shoulders to heels.

3 sets45–60 secBodyweight

Brace hard

DB Russian Twist

Rotate the torso side to side under control while keeping the core braced.

3 sets20 reps (10/side) reps

Feet off ground

Cool-Down / Stretch
  • Half-kneeling hip flexor stretch for 30 seconds per side.
  • Standing quad stretch plus calf wall stretch for 30 seconds per side.
  • Thread-the-needle shoulder stretch and cobra breathing for 45 seconds.
