Chest + Triceps

Sunday · 2026-06-07 · Limited-Barbell

45–60 min workoutBulk focusProgressive overload

Warm-Up — 5 min

  • Arm circles and shoulder openers — 45 sec each direction
  • Scapular push-ups — 2 × 10
  • Light push-ups or empty-bar press pattern — 2 × 8
  • Two ramp-up sets on the first press before working weight

Workout

Chest

Single-Arm DB Floor Press

Press one dumbbell at a time from the floor while bracing your torso against rotation.

3 sets8–10/side repsAnti-rotation pressing focus

DB Close-Grip Floor Press

Press dumbbells from the floor with tucked elbows to load chest and triceps together.

3 sets8–12 repsChest plus tricep overload

Decline Push-Up (hands elevated on couch)

Put your hands on a couch and press through a lower-chest focused push-up path.

3 sets10–15 repsLower chest bodyweight angleBodyweight

Tempo Push-Up

Lower for three seconds, pause briefly near the bottom, then press up strong.

3 sets8–12 repsSlow eccentric chest controlBodyweight

DB Squeeze Press

Press two dumbbells together over your chest so the pecs stay squeezed the whole set.

3 sets10–12 repsConstant pec tension bonus

Triceps

DB Tate Press

Lower dumbbells toward your chest with elbows flared, then extend back up.

3 sets10–12 repsElbows flare for triceps

Single-Arm Overhead DB Extension

Train each tricep separately by extending one dumbbell overhead at a time.

3 sets10–12/side repsSide-to-side balance

Diamond Push-Up

Place hands close together under your chest and press through a tight-elbow push-up.

3 sets8–15 repsBodyweight triceps burnoutBodyweight

Cool-Down / Stretch — 2 min

  • Doorway pec stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Overhead tricep stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Slow nasal breathing with arms supported — 60 sec
