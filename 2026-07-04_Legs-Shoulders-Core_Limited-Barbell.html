Legs + Shoulders + Core

Saturday, 2026-07-04 · starts 9:00 AM

Limited-Barbell45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbRest: 60–90s isolation, 2–3m compounds

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Bodyweight squats, lunges, and hip circles for 90 seconds
  • Shoulder CARs and light lateral raises before pressing
  • Two ramp-up sets on the first leg movement

Workout

Legs

DB Goblet Squat

Hold one dumbbell at your chest and squat deep with a tall torso.

4 sets10–12 reps

Note: Deep squat, chest up

DB Step-Up

Step onto a sturdy surface with dumbbells, driving through the working leg.

3 sets10/leg reps

Note: Step onto sturdy chair/bench substitute with control

DB Hip Thrust on Couch

Place upper back on a couch and thrust hips upward with a dumbbell on your lap.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Upper back on couch, drive hips up hard

Shoulders

DB Arnold Press

Rotate dumbbells from palms-in to palms-forward as you press overhead.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Rotation throughout

DB Lateral Raise 1.5 Reps

Perform lateral raises with an extra half rep to increase side-delt tension.

3 sets10 reps

Note: Raise up, lower halfway, raise again, then lower fully

DB Shrug

Hold dumbbells and shrug shoulders upward, pausing at the top.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Hold at the top for upper traps

Core

Side Plank

Hold a side plank in a straight line while keeping hips lifted.

3 sets30–45 sec/side repsBodyweight

Note: Oblique and anti-lateral-flexion strength

DB Woodchopper

Move a dumbbell diagonally across your body under control to train rotation.

3 sets12/side reps

Note: Rotational power

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Standing quad and hamstring stretches — 30 seconds/side
  • Figure-four glute stretch — 30 seconds/side
  • Doorway shoulder stretch and slow breathing

Progressive Overload Log

If you hit the top of the rep range with clean form for all sets, increase weight by 5 lb next time.
