Chest + Triceps

Thursday, 2026-07-02 · starts 9:00 AM

Full-Gym45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbRest: 60–90s isolation, 2–3m compounds

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Arm circles, bandless shoulder rolls, and scapular push-ups for 60 seconds
  • Two light ramp-up sets of your first press
  • 10 slow push-ups or wall presses to groove the pattern

Workout

Chest

Barbell Bench Press

Press a barbell from your chest while keeping shoulder blades tight and feet planted.

4 sets6–8 reps

Note: Primary compound — heavy, progressive

Machine Incline Chest Press

Press on an incline machine to load the upper chest with stable support.

3 sets8–12 reps

Note: Stable machine press for upper chest overload

Cable Crossover (high to low)

Pull cables down and across your body to squeeze the lower and inner chest.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: High-to-low cable path for lower/inner chest

Pec Deck Machine

Bring the pec deck handles together with a hard chest squeeze at the middle.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Squeeze hard at peak

Triceps

Close-Grip Bench Press

Bench with a narrower grip and tucked elbows to overload the triceps.

3 sets8–10 reps

Note: Elbows tight to body

Overhead Tricep Extension (cable)

Face away from the cable and extend overhead from a deep tricep stretch.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Full stretch overhead

Reverse-Grip Cable Pushdown

Push the cable down with an underhand grip to emphasize the medial tricep head.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Underhand cable pushdown for medial head

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Doorway chest stretch — 30 seconds/side
  • Overhead tricep stretch — 30 seconds/side
  • Slow nasal breathing while relaxing shoulders for 60 seconds

Progressive Overload Log

If you hit the top of the rep range with clean form for all sets, increase weight by 5 lb next time.
