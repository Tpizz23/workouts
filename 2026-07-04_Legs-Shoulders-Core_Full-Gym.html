Legs + Shoulders + Core

Saturday, 2026-07-04 · starts 9:00 AM

Full-Gym45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbRest: 60–90s isolation, 2–3m compounds

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Bodyweight squats, lunges, and hip circles for 90 seconds
  • Shoulder CARs and light lateral raises before pressing
  • Two ramp-up sets on the first leg movement

Workout

Legs

Leg Press

Push the leg press sled through your heels with a controlled deep bend.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: High foot placement for glutes/hams

Bulgarian Split Squat

Place the rear foot on a bench and squat one leg at a time.

3 sets8–10/leg reps

Note: Rear foot on bench

Leg Curl (lying or seated)

Curl the pad toward you to isolate hamstrings with a slow return.

3 sets12 reps

Note: Full extension, controlled

Leg Press Calf Raise

Press the sled with your toes only to train calves through full range.

3 sets15–20 reps

Note: Calf work on leg press sled

Shoulders

DB Arnold Press

Press dumbbells while rotating from palms-in to palms-forward.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Full rotation

Cable Lateral Raise

Raise one cable handle to the side for steady side-delt tension.

3 sets15 reps

Note: Constant cable tension

Cable Reverse Fly

Pull cables out and back to target rear delts and upper back.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Rear delt constant tension

Core

Hanging Leg Raise

Hang from a bar and raise legs without swinging to train lower abs.

3 sets12–15 repsBodyweight

Note: Strict, no swing

Cable Woodchopper

Rotate a cable diagonally across your body with controlled torso movement.

3 sets12/side reps

Note: Rotational cable core work

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Standing quad and hamstring stretches — 30 seconds/side
  • Figure-four glute stretch — 30 seconds/side
  • Doorway shoulder stretch and slow breathing

Progressive Overload Log

If you hit the top of the rep range with clean form for all sets, increase weight by 5 lb next time.
