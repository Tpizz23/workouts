Back + Biceps

Saturday · 2026-05-30 · Full-Gym

45–60 min workoutBulk focusProgressive overload

Warm-Up — 5 min

  • Cat-cow plus thoracic rotations — 60 sec
  • Hip-hinge drill — 2 × 10
  • Prone W squeezes — 2 × 10
  • Two lighter ramp-up row/pull sets before working weight

Workout

Back

Pull-Up or Weighted Pull-Up

Pull from a dead hang until your chin clears the bar, adding weight if reps are strong.

4 sets6–10 repsFull dead hang to chin over bar

T-Bar Row

Row the T-bar handle toward your torso with a braced body and strong squeeze.

4 sets8–10 repsHeavy row anchor

Straight-Arm Pulldown

Keep arms mostly straight and sweep the cable down using your lats, not your biceps.

3 sets12–15 repsLat isolation

Face Pull (cable, rope)

Pull the rope toward your face with elbows high and rotate externally at the end.

3 sets15 repsRear delt and shoulder health

Biceps

Barbell Curl

Curl a barbell without swinging, squeezing the biceps at the top.

3 sets10–12 repsStrict standing curl

Cable Hammer Curl (rope)

Curl the rope attachment with palms facing each other for brachialis and forearms.

3 sets12–15 repsConstant-tension neutral grip

Preacher Curl (EZ bar or machine)

Brace your upper arms on the preacher pad and curl through a controlled range.

2 sets12 repsNo-cheat isolation

Cool-Down / Stretch — 2 min

  • Child’s pose with lat reach — 45 sec/side
  • Cross-body rear delt stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Forearm/bicep wall stretch — 30 sec/side

Progressive Overload Log

Record working weight or reps. If all sets hit the top of the range cleanly, add 5 lb next time.
