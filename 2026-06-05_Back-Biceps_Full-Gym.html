Back + Biceps

Friday · 2026-06-05 · Full-Gym

45–60 min workoutBulk focusProgressive overload

Warm-Up — 5 min

  • Cat-cow and thoracic rotations — 60 sec
  • Hip-hinge patterning — 2 × 10
  • Scapular retractions or light rows — 2 × 12
  • One lighter ramp-up set before the first heavy row or pull

Workout

Back

Pull-Up or Weighted Pull-Up

Pull from a dead hang until your chin clears the bar with full control.

4 sets6–10 repsVertical pull anchorBodyweight

Barbell Bent-Over Row

Hinge over and row the bar toward your torso with a strong back position.

4 sets8–10 repsHeavy horizontal pull

Seated Cable Row (V-bar)

Pull the V-handle to your torso and squeeze shoulder blades together.

3 sets10–12 repsMid-back squeeze

Face Pull (cable, rope)

Pull the rope toward your face with elbows high to train rear delts and rotators.

3 sets15 repsRear delt and shoulder health

Biceps

Barbell Curl

Curl a barbell strictly from straight arms to full bicep squeeze.

3 sets10–12 repsStrict curl anchor

Incline DB Curl

Curl dumbbells from an incline bench to load the biceps from a stretched position.

3 sets12 repsLong-head stretch

Cable Curl (straight bar)

Curl a straight cable bar with constant tension throughout the rep.

3 sets12–15 repsConstant tension

Cool-Down / Stretch — 2 min

  • Child’s pose lat stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Cross-body rear delt stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Gentle bicep wall stretch — 30 sec/side
