Warm-Up — 5 min

  • One lighter ramp-up set before the first heavy row or pull

Workout

Back

DB Bent-Over Row

Hinge at the hips and row both dumbbells toward your ribs with your chest up.

4 sets8–10 repsHeavy two-arm row anchor

Single-Arm DB Row

Brace on a bench and row a dumbbell toward your hip through a full range.

3 sets10–12 repsBrace and pull toward hip

DB Romanian Deadlift

Hinge your hips back with a soft knee bend until hamstrings stretch, then stand tall.

3 sets10–12 repsPosterior-chain hinge

Reverse Snow Angel (prone)

Lie face down and sweep your arms in a snow-angel path to hit upper back.

3 sets12 repsBodyweight upper-back controlBodyweight

Biceps

DB Curl

Curl dumbbells up while turning palms toward you and controlling the lowering phase.

3 sets10–12 repsStrict supinating curl

DB Hammer Curl

Curl with palms facing each other to load the brachialis and forearms.

3 sets12–15 repsNeutral-grip brachialis work

DB Concentration Curl

Curl one dumbbell with your elbow braced for strict peak contraction.

3 sets12 repsStrict peak contraction

  • Cross-body rear delt stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Gentle bicep wall stretch — 30 sec/side
