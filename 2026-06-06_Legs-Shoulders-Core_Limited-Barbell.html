Legs + Shoulders + Core

Saturday · 2026-06-06 · Limited-Barbell

45–60 min workoutBulk focusProgressive overload

Warm-Up — 5 min

  • Bodyweight squats and hip openers — 2 min
  • Glute bridges — 2 × 12
  • Shoulder circles plus light lateral raises — 60 sec
  • Brace practice: dead bug or plank breathing — 60 sec

Workout

Legs

DB Front Squat

Hold two dumbbells at shoulder height and squat with quad-focused posture.

4 sets8–12 repsQuad-focused squat anchor

DB Step-Up

Step onto a sturdy chair or bench substitute and control the descent each rep.

3 sets10/leg repsUnilateral quad and glute work

DB Sumo Deadlift

Hinge from a wide stance and stand tall by squeezing glutes and adductors.

3 sets10–12 repsGlutes and adductors

Wall Sit with DB

Hold a squat against the wall with a dumbbell for a quad burn.

3 sets30–45 sec repsQuad burn finisher

Shoulders

Seated DB Shoulder Press

Press dumbbells overhead from a seated position to reduce leg drive.

4 sets8–10 repsStrict overhead press anchor

Lean-Away DB Lateral Raise

Lean from a support and raise one dumbbell through a longer side-delt range.

3 sets10–12/side repsLong-tension side delts

Prone Rear Delt Y-Raise

Lie face down and lift arms in a Y shape to train rear delts and lower traps.

3 sets12 repsRear delt controlBodyweight

Core

DB Suitcase Carry/March

Hold one heavy dumbbell at your side and march while resisting leaning.

3 sets30 sec/side repsAnti-side-bend core

Plank Shoulder Tap

Tap opposite shoulders from a plank while keeping hips from rotating.

3 sets20 taps repsAnti-rotation plank workBodyweight

Cool-Down / Stretch — 2 min

  • Couch or quad stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Hamstring fold — 45 sec/side
  • Cross-body shoulder stretch and slow breathing — 60 sec

Progressive Overload Log

Record working weight or reps. If all sets hit the top of the range cleanly, add 5 lb next time.
