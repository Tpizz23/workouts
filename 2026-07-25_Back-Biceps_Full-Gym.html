Back + Biceps

Full-Gym45–60 minBulk focus

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • → 2 min brisk walk or easy row
  • → Band-free scapular circles and arm swings
  • → 2 light sets of rows, then 1 easy curl set

Workout

Back

Barbell Bent-Over Row

Hinge forward with a flat back and pull the bar toward your lower ribs to build the mid-back and lats.

4 sets8–10

Pull-Up or Weighted Pull-Up

Hang from the bar and pull your chest upward by driving your elbows down to train the lats and upper back.

4 sets6–10Bodyweight

Seated Cable Row (V-bar)

Sit tall and pull the close handle toward your torso, squeezing your shoulder blades together.

3 sets10–12

Straight-Arm Pulldown

Keep your elbows softly bent as you pull a cable bar from overhead to your thighs using your lats.

3 sets12–15

Face Pull (cable, rope)

Pull the rope toward eye level while rotating your hands back to strengthen rear delts and upper-back posture.

3 sets15

Biceps

Barbell Curl

Curl the bar upward without swinging your torso, then lower it slowly to load the biceps.

3 sets10–12

Incline DB Curl

Curl dumbbells while lying on an incline bench to train the biceps from a long, stretched position.

3 sets12

Cable Hammer Curl (rope)

Curl the rope with neutral palms to target the brachialis and forearms alongside the biceps.

3 sets12–15

Rest 2–3 min on the first compound movement; 60–90 sec on accessory work. Own each controlled rep.

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • ❄ Child’s-pose lat stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Cross-body rear-delt stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Gentle biceps wall stretch — 30 sec/side

Progressive Overload Log

When every set reaches the top of its range with clean form, add 5 lb next time.
