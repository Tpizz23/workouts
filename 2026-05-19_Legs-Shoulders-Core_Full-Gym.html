Legs + Shoulders + Core

2026-05-19 · Tuesday at 9:00 AM

Full-Gym45–60 minBulk: 165 → 180 lbTuesday · 9:00 AMVariant B

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • ➜ 3 minutes incline treadmill walk or easy bike
  • ➜ Leg swings x 10 each direction per leg
  • ➜ Empty-bar squat x 10 and hip hinge x 10
  • ➜ Cable or band face pulls x 15 for shoulder prep

Workout

Legs

Leg Press

A sled press that lets you load quads and glutes hard with stable support.

3 sets10–12 reps

High enough foot placement to protect knees; full controlled reps.

Bulgarian Split Squat

A rear-foot-elevated single-leg squat that builds quads, glutes, and balance.

3 sets8–10/leg reps

Keep torso slightly forward and control depth.

Cable Pull-Through

A cable hip hinge that keeps steady tension on glutes and hamstrings.

3 sets12–15 reps

Hinge back, then squeeze glutes through the top.

Leg Extension

A quad isolation machine that finishes the thighs with controlled tension.

3 sets12–15 reps

Pause and squeeze at the top of each rep.

Leg Press Calf Raise

A calf raise performed on the leg press sled for loaded lower-leg volume.

3 sets15–20 reps

Full stretch at bottom, hard squeeze at top.

Shoulders

Machine Shoulder Press

A stable machine press that builds shoulder strength after leg compounds.

3 sets10–12 reps

Controlled reps without bouncing the stack.

Cable Lateral Raise

A constant-tension cable raise for side-delt width.

3 sets15 reps

Raise just above shoulder height and lower slowly.

Face Pull to External Rotation

A cable pull that trains rear delts and rotator cuff control.

3 sets12–15 reps

Pull to face level, rotate, and squeeze shoulder blades.

Core

Pallof Press (cable)

An anti-rotation cable press that teaches the core to resist twisting.

3 sets12/side reps

Lock ribs down and press straight out.

Ab Wheel Rollout

A rollout that trains abs to resist spinal extension under load.

3 sets8–12 reps

Only roll as far as you can keep a neutral spine.

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • ❄ Couch stretch — 45 sec/side
  • ❄ Seated hamstring stretch — 45 sec/side
  • ❄ Cross-body shoulder stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Kneeling lat/child’s pose reach — 45 sec

Progressive Overload Log

If you hit the top of the rep range with clean form on every set, add 5 lb next time.
