Chest + Triceps

Tuesday, 2026-07-07 · starts 3:30 PM

Limited-Barbell45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbRest: 60–90s isolation, 2–3m compounds

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Arm circles, bandless shoulder dislocates, and scapular push-ups for 90 seconds
  • Two ramp-up press sets before working weight
  • Light close-grip push-ups/extensions to warm elbows

Workout

Chest

Single-Arm DB Floor Press

Press one dumbbell at a time from the floor while bracing your torso against rotation.

3 sets8–10/side reps

Note: Anti-rotation core challenge while pressing

Deficit Push-Up on DB Handles

Place hands on dumbbells and lower deeper than a regular push-up for a bigger chest stretch.

3 sets8–12 repsBodyweight

Note: Hands on dumbbells for a deeper chest stretch

DB Squeeze Press

Press two dumbbells together as you push up to keep constant pec tension.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Press dumbbells together to keep constant pec tension

DB Pullover (floor)

Lower a dumbbell behind your head from the floor and pull it back over your chest.

3 sets12 reps

Note: Chest/serratus stretch

DB Floor Fly with Pause

Open dumbbells wide on the floor, pause near the bottom, then squeeze the chest to return.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Pause one second near the bottom before squeezing up

Triceps

DB Rolling Tricep Extension

Roll dumbbells back beside your head, then extend your elbows to lockout.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Roll dumbbells back then extend for a bigger stretch

Diamond Push-Up

Use a close diamond hand position to make the triceps drive the push-up.

3 sets8–15 repsBodyweight

Note: Hands close together to bias triceps

Single-Arm Overhead DB Extension

Lower one dumbbell behind your head and extend up one arm at a time.

3 sets10–12/side reps

Note: One-arm overhead isolation for side-to-side balance

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Doorway pec stretch, 30 seconds per side
  • Overhead triceps stretch, 30 seconds per side
  • Slow nasal breathing with chest openers for 60 seconds

Progressive Overload Log

When all sets hit the top of the rep range with clean form, increase the load by 5 lb next time.
