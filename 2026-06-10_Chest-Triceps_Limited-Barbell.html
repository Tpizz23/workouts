Chest + Triceps

Limited-Barbell45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbProgressive overload focus

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Arm circles and bandless shoulder rolls — 45 sec each direction
  • Scapular push-ups — 2 × 10
  • Light press warm-up — 2 easy ramp sets
  • Wrist/elbow circles — 30 sec each

Workout

Chest

Single-Arm DB Floor Press

Press one dumbbell at a time while bracing your torso to resist rotation.

3 sets8–10/side

Unilateral press anchor with anti-rotation.

Deficit Push-Up on DB Handles

Bodyweight

Grip dumbbells as handles so your chest can drop slightly lower for a deeper stretch.

3 sets8–12

Deep-stretch bodyweight press.

DB Floor Fly with Pause

Pause near the bottom of each floor fly before squeezing the dumbbells back above the chest.

3 sets10–12

Pause near the stretch.

DB Squeeze Press

Press two dumbbells together while pushing up to maintain constant pec tension.

3 sets10–12

Constant pec tension bonus.

Triceps

DB Rolling Tricep Extension

Roll dumbbells back near your head, then extend hard for a stretched triceps contraction.

3 sets10–12

Stretch then extend.

Single-Arm Overhead DB Extension

Train one triceps side at a time by extending a dumbbell from behind your head to overhead.

3 sets10–12/side

Balance each side.

Diamond Push-Up

Bodyweight

Make a diamond shape with your hands and press through a triceps-heavy push-up.

3 sets8–15

Triceps-heavy bodyweight finisher.

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Doorway chest stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Overhead triceps stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Deep nasal breathing — 1 min

Progressive Overload Log

Log weight by set. When you hit the top of the rep range with clean form across all sets, add 5 lb next time.
