Workout

Chest

Barbell Bench Press

A heavy barbell press that anchors chest strength and progressive overload.

4 sets6–8 reps

Heavy progressive-overload anchor.

Machine Incline Chest Press

A stable incline machine press that overloads upper chest without balance demands.

3 sets8–12 reps

Stable upper-chest overload.

Cable Crossover (high to low)

A high-to-low cable fly that emphasizes lower and inner chest contraction.

3 sets12–15 reps

Lower/inner chest squeeze.

Pec Deck Machine

A machine fly that isolates the pecs with a strong squeeze at peak contraction.

3 sets12–15 reps

Controlled pec isolation.

Weighted Dips (chest lean)

A forward-leaning dip that loads chest, triceps, and shoulder stability.

3 sets8–12 reps

Bonus chest movement with a forward lean.

Triceps

Close-Grip Bench Press

A narrow-grip bench variation that builds triceps strength with pressing carryover.

3 sets8–10 reps

Heavy triceps press with chest carryover.

Overhead Tricep Extension (cable)

A cable overhead extension that loads the long head of the triceps.

3 sets12–15 reps

Long-head cable stretch.

Reverse-Grip Cable Pushdown

An underhand pushdown that emphasizes the medial triceps head.

3 sets12–15 reps

Underhand pushdown for medial head detail.

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • ❄ Doorway pec stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Overhead triceps stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Slow nasal breathing while shaking arms loose for 60 sec
