Workout

Chest

DB Floor Press

A heavy dumbbell press from the floor that builds chest strength while limiting shoulder stress.

4 sets8–10 reps

Primary strength anchor — go heavy with clean reps.

Single-Arm DB Floor Press

A one-arm press that builds chest strength while forcing the core to resist rotation.

3 sets8–10/side reps

Unilateral press for chest and anti-rotation control.

Deficit Push-Up on DB Handles

Bodyweight

A deeper push-up using dumbbell handles to increase the chest stretch at the bottom.

3 sets8–12 reps

Deep-stretch chest volume; stop one rep before form breaks.

DB Floor Fly with Pause

A paused dumbbell fly that reinforces control and a strong pec squeeze.

3 sets10–12 reps

Pause at the stretch, then squeeze up.

Tempo Push-Up

Bodyweight

A slow-lowering push-up that increases time under tension and makes bodyweight reps harder.

3 sets8–12 reps

Bonus chest finisher with a three-second lower.

Triceps

DB Rolling Tricep Extension

A rolling extension that adds stretch before a powerful triceps lockout.

3 sets10–12 reps

Use the roll for stretch, then punch to lockout.

Single-Arm Overhead DB Extension

A one-arm overhead extension that balances triceps strength side to side.

3 sets10–12/side reps

Train each arm evenly through a long stretch.

Diamond Push-Up

Bodyweight

A close-hand push-up that heavily targets the triceps and challenges lockout endurance.

3 sets8–15 reps

Bodyweight lockout finisher for triceps.

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • ❄ Doorway pec stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Overhead triceps stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Slow nasal breathing while shaking arms loose for 60 sec

Progressive Overload Log
