Back + Biceps

Limited-Barbell45–60 minBulk focus

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • → 2 min brisk walk or easy row
  • → Band-free scapular circles and arm swings
  • → 2 light sets of rows, then 1 easy curl set

Workout

Back

DB Bent-Over Row

Hinge at the hips with a flat back and row both dumbbells toward your hips to train the lats and mid-back.

4 sets8–10

Single-Arm DB Row

Brace on a chair or couch and pull one dumbbell toward your hip for a long, controlled lat contraction.

3 sets10–12/side

DB Row with 2-Second Squeeze

Row the dumbbells up and pause with elbows back for two seconds to emphasize the mid-back.

3 sets10–12

Prone Y-T-W Raise

Lying face-down, raise your arms into Y, T, and W shapes to build upper-back and shoulder control.

3 sets8 each shapeBodyweight

Biceps

DB Curl

Curl the dumbbells with palms turning upward, keeping your upper arms still for direct biceps work.

3 sets10–12

Cross-Body Hammer Curl

Curl each dumbbell across your body toward the opposite shoulder to emphasize the brachialis.

3 sets10–12/side

DB Zottman Curl

Curl up palms-up, rotate palms-down at the top, and lower slowly to train biceps and forearms.

3 sets10–12

Rest 2–3 min on the first compound movement; 60–90 sec on accessory work. Own each controlled rep.

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • ❄ Child’s-pose lat stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Cross-body rear-delt stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Gentle biceps wall stretch — 30 sec/side

Progressive Overload Log

When every set reaches the top of its range with clean form, add 5 lb next time.
