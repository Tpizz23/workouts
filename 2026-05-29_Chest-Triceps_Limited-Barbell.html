Chest + Triceps

Friday · 2026-05-29 · Limited-Barbell

45–60 min workoutBulk focusProgressive overload

Warm-Up — 5 min

  • Arm circles and bandless shoulder openers — 45 sec each direction
  • Scapular push-ups — 2 × 10
  • Light push-ups — 2 × 8
  • Two ramp-up sets on the first press before working weight

Workout

Chest

DB Floor Press

Lie on the floor and press dumbbells from chest level to locked-out arms while keeping control.

4 sets8–10 repsPrimary compound — go heavy

Single-Arm DB Floor Press

Press one dumbbell at a time from the floor while bracing your torso against rotation.

3 sets8–10/side repsAnti-rotation core challenge while pressing

DB Squeeze Press

Press two dumbbells together over your chest so the pecs stay squeezed the whole set.

3 sets10–12 repsConstant pec tension

Deficit Push-Up on DB Handles

Use dumbbells as handles so your chest can lower slightly deeper than a normal push-up.

3 sets8–12 repsDeeper chest stretch

DB Floor Fly with Pause

Open the dumbbells wide on the floor, pause in the stretch, then squeeze them back together.

3 sets10–12 repsPause near bottom

Triceps

DB Rolling Tricep Extension

Roll the dumbbells back beside your head, then extend your elbows to lock out.

3 sets10–12 repsBigger stretch

Single-Arm Overhead DB Extension

Hold one dumbbell overhead and bend only the elbow to train each tricep separately.

3 sets10–12/side repsSide-to-side balance

Diamond Push-Up

Place hands close together under your chest and press through a tight-elbow push-up.

3 sets8–15 repsTriceps bodyweight finisherBodyweight

Cool-Down / Stretch — 2 min

  • Doorway pec stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Overhead tricep stretch — 45 sec/side
  • Slow nasal breathing with arms supported — 60 sec
