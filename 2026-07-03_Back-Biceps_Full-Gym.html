Back + Biceps

Friday, 2026-07-03 · starts 9:00 AM

Full-Gym45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbRest: 60–90s isolation, 2–3m compounds

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Hip hinges, cat-cow, and thoracic rotations for 60 seconds
  • Two easy row/pulldown ramp sets before working weight
  • Light curls plus scapular retractions to warm elbows and upper back

Workout

Back

T-Bar Row

Row a T-bar or landmine handle toward your torso with a braced hinge.

4 sets8–10 reps

Note: Heavy supported or landmine row variation

Neutral-Grip Lat Pulldown

Pull a neutral-grip handle to your upper chest while driving elbows down.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Pulldown variation with neutral handle

Chest-Supported DB Row

Lie chest-down on an incline bench and row dumbbells with strict control.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Incline bench row to reduce momentum

Straight-Arm Pulldown

Keep arms mostly straight and sweep the cable down using your lats.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Lat isolation without biceps takeover

Biceps

Barbell Curl

Curl a barbell without swinging, keeping elbows pinned near your sides.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Standing, strict form

Cable Hammer Curl (rope)

Curl a rope attachment with a neutral grip for brachialis and forearms.

3 sets12–15 reps

Note: Constant-tension neutral-grip curl

Preacher Curl (EZ bar or machine)

Curl on a preacher pad to lock the upper arms and isolate biceps.

2 sets12 reps

Note: Isolation, no cheating

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Child’s pose with side reach — 30 seconds/side
  • Cross-body lat stretch and forearm flexor stretch
  • Easy hanging or supported lat stretch if available

Progressive Overload Log

If you hit the top of the rep range with clean form for all sets, increase weight by 5 lb next time.
