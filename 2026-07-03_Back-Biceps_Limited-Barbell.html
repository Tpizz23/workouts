Back + Biceps

Friday, 2026-07-03 · starts 9:00 AM

Limited-Barbell45–60 min workoutBulk: 165 → 180 lbRest: 60–90s isolation, 2–3m compounds

Warm-Up · 5 min

  • Hip hinges, cat-cow, and thoracic rotations for 60 seconds
  • Two easy row/pulldown ramp sets before working weight
  • Light curls plus scapular retractions to warm elbows and upper back

Workout

Back

Chest-Supported DB Row on Couch

Brace chest on a couch angle and row dumbbells without swinging your torso.

4 sets8–12 reps

Note: Lie chest-down on an incline couch/bench substitute

DB Pullover Lat Focus

Move a dumbbell from behind your head to over your chest while driving from the lats.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Keep elbows soft and pull from lats

Single-Arm DB Rear Delt Row

Row one dumbbell out wide toward your shoulder to train upper back and rear delts.

3 sets12/side reps

Note: Row wide toward shoulder to hit rear delts

Prone Y-T-W Raise

Lie face-down and raise your arms into Y, T, and W shapes with control.

3 sets8 each shape reps

Note: Bodyweight upper-back and shoulder control

Biceps

DB Zottman Curl

Curl palms-up, rotate at the top, and lower palms-down to hit biceps and forearms.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Curl up supinated, lower palms-down

Cross-Body Hammer Curl

Curl each dumbbell across your torso toward the opposite shoulder with a neutral grip.

3 sets10–12/side reps

Note: Curl toward opposite shoulder for brachialis emphasis

DB Drag Curl

Drag dumbbells up the front of your body while pulling elbows behind you.

3 sets10–12 reps

Note: Pull elbows back as the dumbbells travel up torso

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • Child’s pose with side reach — 30 seconds/side
  • Cross-body lat stretch and forearm flexor stretch
  • Easy hanging or supported lat stretch if available

Progressive Overload Log

If you hit the top of the rep range with clean form for all sets, increase weight by 5 lb next time.
