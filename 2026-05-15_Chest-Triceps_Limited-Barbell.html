  • ➜ Light push-ups x 8–10

Workout

Chest

DB Floor Press

A heavy dumbbell press from the floor that builds chest strength while limiting shoulder stress.

4 sets8–10 reps

Primary strength anchor — beat last session by one rep if possible.

DB Squeeze Press

A dumbbell press where you squeeze the weights together to keep constant pec tension.

3 sets10–12 reps

Constant pec tension from first rep to last.

Incline Push-Up (feet elevated)

Bodyweight

A feet-elevated push-up that shifts more demand toward the upper chest and shoulders.

3 sets10–15 reps

Upper-chest bodyweight volume.

DB Pullover (floor)

A floor pullover that stretches the chest and lats while training serratus control.

3 sets12 reps

Stretch chest and serratus while staying controlled.

DB Close-Grip Floor Press

A tucked-elbow floor press that overloads triceps while still challenging the chest.

3 sets8–12 reps

Unique chest bonus with triceps carryover.

Triceps

DB Skull Crusher (floor)

A lying dumbbell extension that isolates the triceps with elbows kept steady.

3 sets10–12 reps

Keep elbows stacked and move only at the elbow.

DB Overhead Tricep Extension

An overhead extension that trains the long head of the triceps through a big stretch.

3 sets12–15 reps

Long-head stretch work.

Close-Grip Push-Up

Bodyweight

A narrow-hand push-up that shifts the work toward triceps lockout strength.

3 sets10–15 reps

Finish with tight elbows and clean lockout.

Cool-Down / Stretch · 2 min

  • ❄ Doorway pec stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Overhead triceps stretch — 30 sec/side
  • ❄ Slow nasal breathing while shaking arms loose for 60 sec

Progressive Overload Log

If you hit the top of the rep range with clean form for all sets, add 5 lb next time.
