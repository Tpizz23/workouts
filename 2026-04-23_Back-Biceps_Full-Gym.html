Back + Biceps
Build size with purpose today — strong compounds first, then clean hypertrophy work to finish the target muscles.
Equipment: Full-GymDuration: 45–60 minGoal: Bulk · Chest priorityStart: 9:00 AM
Warm-Up
  • 5 minutes on the rower plus banded face pulls and straight-arm pulldowns for 2 rounds.
  • 1 ramp-up set each for pull-ups/pulldowns and rows.
  • 10 scap pull-ups or bar hangs to groove shoulder positioning.
Workout

Back

Pull-Up or Weighted Pull-Up

Start from a dead hang and pull until the chin clears the bar without kicking.

4 sets6–10 reps

Full dead hang to chin over bar

Barbell Bent-Over Row

Hinge over with a flat torso and row the bar toward the lower chest or upper abs.

4 sets8–10 reps

Overhand, chest up, big pull

Seated Cable Row (V-bar)

Drive the elbows back and squeeze the mid-back before returning under control.

3 sets10–12 reps

Drive elbows back, squeeze

Lat Pulldown (wide grip)

Pull the bar to the upper chest while keeping the ribcage down and shoulders packed.

3 sets10–12 reps

When pull-ups are done

Face Pull (cable, rope)

Pull the rope toward the face with elbows high to train rear delts and rotator cuff.

3 sets15 reps

External rotation, rear delts

Biceps

Barbell Curl

Curl the bar with strict posture and no hip drive so the biceps do the work.

3 sets10–12 reps

Standing, strict form

Incline DB Curl

Let the arms hang behind the body on an incline bench and curl through a long stretch.

3 sets12 reps

Long head stretch on incline bench

Cable Curl (straight bar)

Curl against steady cable tension and squeeze the biceps hard at the top.

3 sets12–15 reps

Constant tension

Cool-Down / Stretch
  • Hang from a pull-up bar for 20–30 seconds if shoulders tolerate it.
  • Lat stretch on a bench plus cross-body shoulder stretch for 30 seconds per side.
  • Open the biceps against a wall for 30 seconds per side.
